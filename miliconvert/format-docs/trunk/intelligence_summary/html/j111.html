FUNCTION OR PURPOSE
:
THE INTSUM IS USED TO PERIODICALLY INFORM ADDRESSEES OF MILITARY, ASSOCIATED POLITICAL/ECONOMICAL OR OTHER RELATED INTELLIGENCE AND THE ASSESSMENT OF THIS.
