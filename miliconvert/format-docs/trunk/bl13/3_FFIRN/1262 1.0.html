1262

 

Field identifier (Name):

1262 (GEOGRAPHICAL ENTITY (2A-3A))

 

 

Definition:

THE INDEPENDENT FIRST-LEVEL GEOGRAPHIC-POLITICAL AREA AND ITS DEPENDENCIES, AREA OF QUASI-INDEPENDENCE, AND AREA WITH SPECIAL UNRECOGNISED SOVEREIGNTY ASSOCIATIONS, SOVEREIGN POLITICAL REGIME AND ADMINISTRATIVE DIVISION WITHOUT SOVEREIGNTY, INCLUDING OUTLYING AND DISPUTED AREAS.

 

 

Sponsor:

None

 

 

Remarks:

THIS FFIRN MUST BE REVIEWED AND UPDATED WITH THE PROMULGATION OF EACH NEW EDITION OF THE RELATED DOCUMENT, STANAG 1059. NATIONAL MG AGREEMENT OF THIS FFIRN DOES NOT ALTER THE RESERVATIONS NATIONS HAVE MADE WITH RESPECT TO THE RELATED DOCUMENT, UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED.

 

 

Related Documents:         

IS0 3661-1, ISO 3661-2, ISO 3661-3, STANAG 1059, EDITION 8

In Service
