1A THROUGH 1Z:  PREPLANNED: MISSION TARGETS CAPABLE OF PREVENTING EXECUTION OF PLAN OF ACTION OR MISSION PURPOSE IS OF PARAMOUNT IMPORTANCE TO EXECUTION OF PLAN OF ACTION.  IMMEDIATE (EMERGENCY):  RESULTS OF THESE REQUESTS ARE OF PARAMOUNT IMPORTANCE TO THE IMMEDIATE BATTLE SITUATION OR OBJECTIVE, E.G., AN ENEMY BREAKTHROUGH.  THESE REQUESTS TAKE PRIORITY OVER ALL OTHER REQUESTS EXCEPT PREVIOUSLY ASSIGNED PRIORITY 1 REQUESTS.  RECONNAISSANCE:  TAKES PRECEDENCE OVER ALL OTHER REQUESTS EXCEPT PREVIOUSLY ASSIGNED PRIORITIES 1.  RESULTS OF THESE REQUESTS ARE OF PARAMOUNT IMPORTANCE TO THE IMMEDIATE BATTLE SITUATION OR OBJECTIVE.

2A THROUGH 2Z:  PREPLANNED: MISSION TARGETS CAPABLE OF IMMEDIATE SERIOUS INTERFERENCE WITH PLAN OF ACTION OR MISSION PURPOSE IS OF ESSENTIAL SUPPORT TO THE GENERAL BATTLE SITUATION AND WILL BE ACCOMPLISHED AS SOON AS POSSIBLE AFTER PRIORITY 1 REQUESTS.  IMMEDIATE (URGENT):  RESULTS OF THESE REQUIREMENTS ARE IN SUPPORT OF THE GENERAL BATTLE SITUATION AND WILL BE ACCOMPLISHED AS SOON AS POSSIBLE AFTER PRIORITY 1 REQUESTS, E.G., ENEMY ARTILLERY OR MORTAR FIRE FALLING ON FRIENDLY TROOPS OR MECHANIZED UNITS MOVING UP IN SUCH FORCE AS TO THREATEN A BREAKTHROUGH.  RECONNAISSANCE:  RESULTS OF THESE REQUIREMENTS ARE IN SUPPORT OF THE GENERAL BATTLE SITUATION AND WILL BE ACCOMPLISHED AS SOON AS POSSIBLE AFTER PRIORITY 1.  THESE ARE REQUESTS TO GAIN CURRENT BATTLE INFORMATION.

3A THROUGH 3Z:  PREPLANNED: MISSION TARGETS CAPABLE OF ULTIMATE SERIOUS INTERFERENCE WITH PLAN OF ACTION OR MISSION PURPOSE IS OF ORDINARY SUPPORT AND DOES NOT SIGNIFICANTLY AFFECT THE IMMEDIATE BATTLE SITUATION.  IMMEDIATE (ORDINARY):  RESULTS OF THESE REQUESTS DO NOT AFFECT THE IMMEDIATE BATTLE SITUATION, BUT THEY TAKE PRIORITY OVER ROUTINE MISSIONS, E.G., A TARGET WHICH IS DELAYING A UNITS ADVANCE BUT WHICH IS NOT CAUSING CASUALTIES.  RECONNAISSANCE:  RESULTS OF THESE REQUESTS UPDATE THE INTELLIGENCE DATA BASE BUT DO NOT AFFECT THE IMMEDIATE BATTLE SITUATION.

4A THROUGH 4Z:  PREPLANNED: MISSION TARGETS CAPABLE OF LIMITED INTERFERENCE WITH EXECUTION OF PLAN OF ACTION OR MISSION PURPOSE IS OF ROUTINE NATURE.  IMMEDIATE (ROUTINE):  RESULTS OF THESE REQUESTS ARE OF A ROUTINE NATURE, E.G., TARGETS WHICH ARE NOT INHIBITING A UNIT'S ADVANCE BUT BY THEIR FLEETING NATURE AND TACTICAL IMPORTANCE SHOULD BE LOCATED AND DESTROYED.  RECONNAISSANCE:  RESULTS OF THESE REQUESTS ARE OF A ROUTINE NATURE.  THESE REQUESTS WILL BE FULFILLED WHEN THE RECONNAISSANCE EFFORT PERMITS.

Table 1076/19 - RECUPERATION REPAIR PRIORITY (1-1)

 

Related Documents:

Sponsor:

Unit of Measure:

 

 

RECUPERATION REPAIR PRIORITY (Data Item)

Data Code

Explanation

TOP PRIORITY

1

HAS HIGHEST PRIORITIES FOR REPAIR, RECONSTRUCTION AND REPLACEMENT OF DAMAGED OR OUTMODED EQUIPMENT.  ENTITY IS SO LOCATED THAT SKILLED TECHNICAL PERSONNEL, LABOR OR EQUIPMENT ARE EASILY OBTAINABLE WHEN NEEDED FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND REPAIRS.

HIGH PRIORITY

3

PRIORITIES ARE AS HIGH AS TOP PRIORITY, BUT ENTITY IS LOCATED FARTHER FROM SOURCES OF EQUIPMENT AND SKILLED MANPOWER, SO THAT REPAIRS WOULD BE SOMEWHAT DELAYED, OR ENTITY PRIORITIES MAY NOT BE AS HIGH AS THOSE IN TOP PRIORITY.

MEDIUM PRIORITY

5

ENTITY IS SUFFICIENTLY REMOVED FROM MANUFACTURING AREAS AND / OR PRIORITIES ARE CONSIDERABLE LOWER THAN IN TOP OR HIGH PRIORITY, SO THAT CONSIDERABLE DELAYS IN OBTAINING SKILLED MANPOWER AND EQUIPMENT MUST BE EXPECTED.

LOW PRIORITY

7

ENTITY OPERATES IN A REMOTE NETWORK AND PRIORITIES FOR EQUIPMENT AND SKILLED MANPOWER ARE VERY LOW.  VERY LONG DELAYS FOR REPLACEMENT AND REPAIRS ARE COMMON.

NO PRIORITY

9

ENTITY IS OPERATING WITH OUTDATED TECHNOLOGY OR LOCATED IN SUBSTANDARD BUILDING STRUCTURES SUCH THAT ANY DAMAGE WOULD RESULT IN SCRAPPING THE ENTITY AND CONSIDERING A TOTAL REBUILD.

UNKNOWN

U

None

INCONCLUSIVE ANALYSIS

Z
