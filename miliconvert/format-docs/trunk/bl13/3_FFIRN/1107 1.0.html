Field identifier (Name):

1107 (ARMED SERVICE)

 

 

Definition:

A MILITARY, PARAMILITARY, OR IRREGULAR FORCE OR GROUP CAPABLE OFFUNCTIONING AS AN OFFENSIVE OR DEFENSIVE COMBAT OR SUPPORTORGANIZATION.
