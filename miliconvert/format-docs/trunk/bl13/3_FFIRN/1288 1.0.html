1288

 

Field identifier (Name):

1288 (SECURITY CLASSIFICATION, ABBREVIATED)

 

 

Definition:

A CATEGORY ASSIGNED TO CLASSIFIED MESSAGE INFORMATION OR MATERIAL TO SHOW THE DEGREE OF DAMAGE TO THE INTERESTS OF NATO, OR NATIONS, WHICH COULD RESULT FROM ITS UNAUTHORIZED DISCLOSURE AND TO SHOW THE STANDARD OF PROTECTION REQUIRED TO GUARD AGAINST UNAUTHORIZED DISCLOSURE.
