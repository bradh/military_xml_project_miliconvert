Field identifier (Name):

1195 (GEODETIC DATUM)

 

 

Definition:

A GEODETIC DATUM IS DEFINED BY AN ELLIPSOID, WHICH APPROXIMATES THE SHAPE OF THE EARTH, AND BY THE RELATIONSHIP OF THE EARTH TO THAT ELLIPSOID, E.G. SPHEROIDAL COORDINATES AT AN ORIGIN.
