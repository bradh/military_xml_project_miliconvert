Data Code

Explanation

HIGH LEVEL

H

NO DEFICIENCY IN EITHER EQUIPMENT OR CREW PROFICIENCY.

MEDIUM LEVEL

M

LOW LEVEL

L

DEFICIENCY IN EQUIPMENT AND/OR CREW PROFICIENCY THAT WOULD PRECLUDE CONDUCT OF OPERATIONS WITHOUT DEFECT RECTIFICATION AND/OR CREW TRAINING.
