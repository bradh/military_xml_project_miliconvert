Field identifier (Name):

1856 (BROADCAST AUTHORITY)

 

 

Definition:

DATA ITEMS AND CODES WHICH INDICATE THE NATION, ALLIANCE OR GROUPING OF NATIONAL FORCES UNDER WHOSE OWNERSHIP A BROADCAST IS CONDUCTED.  THE ALLOCATION OF LETTERS TO NATIONS IS BASED ON THE ALLOCATION PROVIDED BY ACP 121(F), CHAPTER 5 (ROUTING INDICATOR DELINEATION TABLE) (ACP 176(G), PARA 613A).

 

 

Sponsor:

None

 

 

Remarks:

THIS FFIRN MUST BE UPDATED WITH THE PROMULGATION OF EACH NEW EDITION OF ACP 176 AND ACP 121.  UNLESS OTHERWISE NOTED, NATIONAL MG AGREEMENT OF THIS FFIRN DOES NOT ALTER THE RESERVATIONS NATIONS HAVE MADE WITH RESPECT TO THE RELATED DOCUMENTS.

 

 

Related Documents:         

ACP 121(F), CHAPTER 5, ACP 176(G), PARA 613A

 

 

Status:  

In Service
