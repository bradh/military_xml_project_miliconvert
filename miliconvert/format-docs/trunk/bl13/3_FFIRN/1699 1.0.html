Field identifier (Name):

1699 (FORCE CODE)

 

 

Definition:

A NUMBER USED IN NAVAL AUTOMATED SYSTEMS TO IDENTIFY THE NATURE OF SPECIFIED TRACKS.

 

 

Sponsor:

None

 

 

Remarks:

THE APPEARANCE OF "UNKNOWN UNKNOWN" OR "UNKNOWN UNKNOWN UNKNOWN" IN DATA ITEMS IS INTENTIONAL.MTF SYSTEMS ARE NOT PERMITTED TO AUTOMATICALLY ASSIGN DATA CODES 01, 03, 04, 06, 07, 09, 22 AND 24 (EXCEPT TO PRESERVE PREVIOUSLY ASSIGNED LINK 11/16 THREAT AND CATEGORY VALUES RECEIVED DURING LINK 11/16 OPERATIONS).  THIS  RESTRICTION PREVENTS A SYSTEM FROM AUTOMATICALLY VIOLATING RULES OF ENGAGEMENT CRITERIA OR POSITIVE DETERMINATIONS.

 

 

Related Documents:         

NONE
