Table 1395/3 - TYPE OF CONTROL AGENCY (3-4)

 

Related Documents:

Unit of Measure:

 

 

TYPE OF CONTROL AGENCY (Data Item)

Data Code

Explanation

AIR SUPPORT RADAR TEAM

ASRT

A SUBORDINATE OPERATIONAL COMPONENT OF A TACTICAL AIR CONTROL SYSTEM WHICH PROVIDES GROUND CONTROLLED PRECISION FLIGHT PATH GUIDANCE AND WEAPONS RELEASE.

MISSION COORDINATOR

CORD

THE AGENCY OR AIRCRAFT THAT IS IN CHARGE OF A MISSION OR PHASE OF THE MISSION, BUT DOES NOT DIRECT THE AIRCRAFT.

FINAL CONTROL AGENCY

FINL

THE AGENCY THAT DIRECTS AN AIRCRAFT AT THE OBJECTIVE OF THE MISSION. E.G., FORWARD AIR CONTROLLER.

HELICOPTER COORDINATOR (AIRBORNE)

HCA

AN AIRBORNE COORDINATOR OF HELICOPTER OPERATIONS.

HELICOPTER SUPPORT TEAM

HST

THE TEAM WHICH PROVIDES SUPPORT TO HELICOPTER OPERATIONS.

INFLIGHT REPORT MONITOR

INFL

THE AGENCY THAT RECEIVES THE INFLIGHT REPORT.

INITIAL CONTROL AGENCY

INTL

THE AGENCY THAT IS CONTACTED WHEN AN AIRCRAFT FIRST ENTERS ANOTHER SERVICE'S CONTROLLED AIRSPACE.

OPERATIONAL CONTROL AGENCY

OPCN

THE AGENCY THAT EXERCISES OPERATIONAL CONTROL OF A MISSION, I.E., CAN CHANGE THE MISSION OF AN AIRCRAFT.

TACTICAL AIR COORDINATOR, AIRBORNE

TACA

AN OFFICER WHO COORDINATES, FROM AN AIRCRAFT, THE ACTION OF COMBAT AIRCRAFT ENGAGED IN CLOSE SUPPORT OF GROUND OR SEA FORCES.

TYPE OF AIRCRAFT CONTROL AGENCY (Data Item)

Data Code

Explanation

AIR SUPPORT OPERATIONS CENTRE

ASOC

(U.S.) AN AGENCY OF A TACTICAL AIR CONTROL SYSTEM COLLOCATED WITH A CORPS HEADQUARTERS OR AN APPROPRIATE LAND FORCE HEADQUARTERS, WHICH COORDINATES AND DIRECTS CLOSE AIR SUPPORT AND OTHER TACTICAL AIR SUPPORT.

AIR SOVEREIGNITY OPERATIONS CENTRE

AIRSOC

None

TACTICAL AIR CONTROL PARTY

TACP

(U.S.) INTEGRAL PART OF EACH COMBAT UNIT FROM DIVISION DOWN TO BATTALION LEVEL AND SERVES AS AN AIR ADVISOR TO EACH MANEUVER UNIT.  PROVIDES ASSISTANCE IN THE SUBMISSION OF PREPLANNED AND IMMEDIATE AIR SUPPORT REQUESTS, AS WELL AS PROVIDING TERMINAL CONTROL FOR SUPPORTING AIRCRAFT.

(AAP-6) A SUBORDINATE OPERATIONAL COMPONENT OF A TACTICAL AIR CONTROL SYSTEM DESIGNED TO PROVIDE AIR LIAISON TO LAND FORCES AND FOR THE CONTROL OF THE AIRCRAFT.

TACTICAL AIR TRAFFIC CONTROL UNIT

TATCU

(UK) A TATCU PROVIDES A 24 HOURS VISUAL FLIGHT RULES (VFR) AIR TRAFFIC CONTROL (ATC) SERVICE FOR COVERT AND OVERT OPERATIONS IN AUSTERE AND BAREBASE CONDITIONS.

MARINE TACTICAL AIR OPERATIONS CENTRE

TAOC

(U.S.) THE PRINCIPAL AIR CONTROL AGENCY OF THE US MARINE AIR COMMAND AND CONTROL SYSTEM RESPONSIBLE FOR AIRSPACE CONTROL AND MANAGEMENT. IT PROVIDES REAL-TIME SURVEILLANCE, DIRECTION, POSITIVE CONTROL, AND NAVIGATIONAL ASSISTANCE FOR FRIENDLY AIRCRAFT. IT PERFORMS REAL-TIME DIRECTION AND CONTROL OF ALL ANTI-AIR WARFARE OPERATIONS, TO INCLUDE MANNED INTERCEPTORS AND SURFACE-TO-AIR WEAPONS. IT IS SUBORDINATE TO THE TACTICAL AIR COMMAND CENTRE.

MARINE TACTICAL AIR DIRECTION CENTRE

TADC

(U.S.) AN AIR OPERATIONS INSTALLATION UNDER THE OVERALL CONTROL OF THE TACTICAL AIR CONTROL CENTRE (AFLOAT) OR TACTICAL AIR COMMAND CENTRE, FROM WHICH AIRCRAFT AND AIR WARNING SERVICE FUNCTIONS OF TACTICAL AIR OPERATIONS IN AN AREA OF CONCERN ARE DIRECTED.

MARINE TACTICAL AIR COMMAND CENTRE

TACC

(U.S.) THE SENIOR US MARINE COMMAND AND CONTROL AGENCY PROVIDING CENTRALIZED COMMAND FOR MAGTF FORCES, AND SERVES AS THE COMMAND POST FOR THE AVIATION COMBAT ELEMENT (ACE) COMMANDER.

MARINE EARLY WARNING/CONTROL SITE

EWC

(U.S.) US MARINE MANNED SITE THAT IS SUBORDINATE TO THE TACTICAL AIR OPERATIONS CENTRE (TAOC), AND PROVIDES THE TAOC WITH EXTENDED RADAR COVERAGE AND CONTROL OF ANTI-AIR WARFARE AIRCRAFT AND SURFACE-TO-AIR WEAPONS.

AIR CONTROL CENTRE

ACC

None

AIRBORNE EARLY WARNING AIRCRAFT

AEW

None

AIR OPERATIONS CENTRE

AOC

(U.S.) THE PRINCIPAL AIR OPERATIONS INSTALLATION FROM WHICH AIRCRAFT AND AIR WARNING FUNCTIONS OF COMBAT AIR OPERATIONS ARE DIRECTED, CONTROLLED, AND EXECUTED. IT IS THE SENIOR AGENCY OF THE AIR FORCE COMPONENT COMMANDER FROM WHICH COMMAND AND CONTROL OF AIR OPERATIONS ARE COORDINATED WITH OTHER COMPONENTS AND SERVICES.

AIR OPERATIONS CO-ORDINATION CENTRE (LAND)

AOCCL

None

AIR OPERATIONS CO-ORDINATION CENTRE (MARITIME)

AOCCM

None

AIR CONTROL CENTRE (ACC), RECOGNISED AIR PICTURE PRODUCTION CENTRE (RPC), SENSOR FUSION POST (SFP)

ARS

None

ASSAULT SUPPORT COORDINATOR,  AIRBORNE

ASCA

(U.S.) MEMBER OF MARINE AIR COMMAND AND CONTROL SYSTEM (MACCS). PROVIDES AIR COORDINATION AND CONTROL DURING HELICOPTER OPERATIONS.  SERVES AS EXTENSION OF DASC OR HELICOPTER DIRECTION CENTRE (HDC) IN SUPPORT OF THE AIR MISSION COMMANDER.

AIRBORNE WARNING AND CONTROL SYSTEM

AWACS

(U.S.) THE E-3 SENTRY IS AN AIRBORNE WARNING AND CONTROL SYSTEM (AWACS) AIRCRAFT THAT PROVIDES ALL-WEATHER SURVEILLANCE, COMMAND, CONTROL AND COMMUNICATIONS NEEDED BY COMMANDERS OF U.S. AND NATO AIR DEFENSE FORCES.

COMBINED AIR OPERATIONS CENTRE

CAOC

(U.S.) A FACILITY, STAFFED BY MEMBERS OF MORE THAN ONE NATION, ESTABLISHED FOR PLANNING, DIRECTING, AND EXECUTING COMBINED AIR OPERATIONS IN SUPPORT OF THE COMBINED FORCE COMMANDER'S OPERATION OR CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES.

COMBINED AIR OPERATIONS CENTRE (CAOC), AIR CONTROL CENTRE (ACC), RECOGNISED AIR PICTURE PRODUCTION CENTRE (RPC), SENSOR FUSION POST (SFP)

CARS

None

COMBAT CONTROL TEAM

CCT

(U.S.) A SMALL TASK ORGANIZED TEAM OF PARACHUTE AND COMBAT DIVER QUALIFIED PERSONNEL TRAINED AND EQUIPPED TO RAPIDLY ESTABLISH AND CONTROL DROP, LANDING, AND EXTRACTION ZONE AIR TRAFFIC IN AUSTERE OR HOSTILE CONDITIONS. THEY SURVEY AND ESTABLISH TERMINAL AIRHEADS AS WELL AS PROVIDE GUIDANCE TO AIRCRAFT FOR AIRLIFT OPERATIONS. THEY PROVIDE COMMAND AND CONTROL, AND CONDUCT RECONNAISSANCE, SURVEILLANCE, AND SURVEY ASSESSMENTS OF POTENTIAL OBJECTIVE AIRFIELDS OR ASSAULT ZONES.

COMMAND AND CONTROL MOBILE

C2M

None

CONTROL AND REPORTING CENTRE

CRC

(U.S.) THE CRC SERVES AS THE SENIOR GROUND RADAR ELEMENT OF THE THEATER AIR CONTROL SYSTEM (TACS).  MISSION IS TO DETECT, IDENTIFY, AND VERIFY AIRBORNE TARGETS IN ASSIGNED AREA.  CRC ALSO PROVIDES DECENTRALIZED EXECUTION OF AIR DEFENSE AND AIRSPACE CONTROL, PROVIDES EARLY WARNING TO AIR AND GROUND ELEMENTS AND MANAGES SUBORDINATE RADAR UNITS.

CONTROL AND REPORTING POST

DIRECT AIR SUPPORT CENTRE, AIRBORNE

DASA

(U.S.) AN AIRBORNE AIRCRAFT EQUIPPED WITH THE NECESSARY STAFF PERSONNEL, COMMUNICATIONS, AND OPERATIONS FACILITIES TO FUNCTION AS A DIRECT AIR SUPPORT CENTRE.

DIRECT AIR SUPPORT CENTRE

DASC

(U.S.) THE PRINCIPAL AIR CONTROL AGENCY OF THE US MARINE AIR COMMAND AND CONTROL SYSTEM RESPONSIBLE FOR THE DIRECTION AND CONTROL OF AIR OPERATIONS DIRECTLY SUPPORTING THE GROUND COMBAT ELEMENT. IT PROCESSES AND COORDINATES REQUESTS FOR IMMEDIATE AIR SUPPORT AND COORDINATES AIR MISSIONS REQUIRING INTEGRATION WITH GROUND FORCES AND OTHER SUPPORTING ARMS. IT NORMALLY COLLOCATES WITH THE SENIOR FIRE SUPPORT COORDINATION CENTER WITHIN THE GROUND COMBAT ELEMENT AND IS SUBORDINATE TO THE TACTICAL AIR COMMAND CENTRE.

DEPLOYABLE CARS

None

FORWARD AIR CONTROLLER

FAC

(U.S.) A MEMBER OF THE TACTICAL AIR CONTROL PARTY WHO, FROM A FORWARD GROUND OR AIRBORNE POSITION, CONTROLS AIRCRAFT IN CLOSE AIR SUPPORT OF GROUND TROOPS.

(AAP-6) A QUALIFIED INDIVIDUAL WHO, FROM A FORWARD POSITION ON THE GROUND OR IN THE AIR, DIRECTS THE ACTION OF COMBAT AIRCRAFT ENGAGED IN CLOSE AIR SUPPORT OF LAND FORCES.

FORWARD AIR CONTROLLER, AIRBORNE

FACA

(U.S.) A SPECIFICALLY TRAINED AND QUALIFIED AVIATION OFFICER WHO EXERCISES CONTROL FROM THE AIR OF AIRCRAFT ENGAGED IN CLOSE AIR SUPPORT OF GROUND TROOPS. THE FORWARD AIR CONTROLLER (AIRBORNE) IS NORMALLY AN AIRBORNE EXTENSION OF THE TACTICAL AIR CONTROL PARTY.

FORWARD AIR CONTROL PARTY

HELICOPTER SUPPORT TEAM

HST

(U.S.) A TASK ORGANIZATION FORMED AND EQUIPPED FOR EMPLOYMENT IN A LANDING ZONE TO FACILITATE THE LANDING AND MOVEMENT OF HELICOPTER-BORNE TROOPS, EQUIPMENT, AND SUPPLIES, AND TO EVACUATE SELECTED CASUALTIES AND ENEMY PRISONERS OF WAR.

INITIAL TERMINAL GUIDANCE TEAM

ITG

(U.S.) ELECTRONIC, MECHANICAL, VISUAL, OR OTHER ASSISTANCE GIVEN AN AIRCRAFT PILOT TO FACILITATE ARRIVAL AT, OPERATION WITHIN OR OVER, LANDING UPON, OR DEPARTURE FROM AN AIR LANDING OR AIRDROP FACILITY.

JOINT AIR OPERATIONS CENTRE

JAOC

(U.S.) A JOINTLY STAFFED FACILITY ESTABLISHED FOR PLANNING, DIRECTING, AND EXECUTING JOINT AIR OPERATIONS IN SUPPORT OF THE JOINT FORCE COMMANDER'S OPERATION OR CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES.

MARINE AIR TRAFFIC CONTROL DETACHMENT

MATC

(U.S.) SUBORDINATE TO THE US MARINE TACC, THE MATC CONTROLS LOCAL AIRSPACE/AIRCRAFT IN AND AROUND A MARINE AIR GROUND TASK FORCE (MAGTF) AIRFIELDS AND PROVIDES PRECISION APPROACH CONTROL FOR MAGTF AIRCRAFT.
