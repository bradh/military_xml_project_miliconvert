Field identifier (Name):

1621 (DESIGNATION OF EMISSIONS)

 

 

Definition:

THE STANDARD INTERNATIONAL TELECOMMUNICATIONS UNION CODE DESCRIBING THE CHARACTERISTICS OF A RADIO EMISSION.

 

 

Sponsor:

None

 

 

Remarks:

THE SPECIFIC DATA ITEMS AND CODES FOR THIS FFIRN ARE LISTED IN A NATO CONFIDENTIAL PUBLICATION BUT BY THEMSELVES ARE CONSIDERED NATO UNCLASSIFIED.

 

 

Related Documents:         

ACP 176 NATO SUPP-1(D)

 

 

Status:  

In Service
