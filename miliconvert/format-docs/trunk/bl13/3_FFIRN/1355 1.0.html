Unit of Measure:

 

 

AIRLIFT PRIORITY INDICATOR (Data Item)

Data Code

Explanation

PRIORITY 1

1

THE PRIORITY ASSIGNED TO THE MOVEMENT OF AIR TRAFFIC REQUIRED BY AN EMERGENCY SO ACUTE THAT PRECEDENCE SHOULD BE GIVEN OVER ALL OTHER AIR TRAFFIC. TRAFFIC OF THIS TYPE IS RESERVED FOR REQUIREMENTS OF UTMOST URGENCY AND IMPORTANCE, AND SHOULD NOT BE DELAYED ENROUTE UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES. THE USE OF THIS CLASSIFICATION SHOULD BE LIMITED TO A RELATIVELY SMALL AMOUNT OF AIR TRAFFIC AND SHALL BE AUTHORIZED ONLY AFTER CAREFUL REVIEW BY THE ISSUING AUTHORITY DETERMINES THAT THE DEGREE OF URGENCY WARRANTS USE OF THIS PRIORITY. NOTE: CERTAIN AIR TRAFFIC IS FURTHER SUBDIVIDED INTO PRIORITIES 1A AND 1B.

PRIORITY 1A

1A

PRIORITY 1 AIR TRAFFIC IN SUPPORT OF MNC-DIRECTED MISSIONS, ALLIED FORCES IN COMBAT, AND SPECIAL WEAPONS.

PRIORITY 1B

1B

PRIORITY 1 AIR TRAFFIC IN SUPPORT OF THE MOVEMENT OF ALLIED FORCES INTO IMMEDIATE COMBAT.

PRIORITY 2

2

TRANSPORTATION BY AIR OF CARGO, PERSONNEL, OR MAIL DETERMINED TO BE OF AN URGENT NATURE IN ORDER TO MEET DEADLINES IN THE ACCOMPLISHMENT OF AN ESSENTIAL MISSION. COVERS AIR TRAFFIC IN SUPPORT OF ALLIED CONTINGENCY OPERATIONS, MOVEMENT OF ACE MOBILE FORCES, AND RAPID REINFORCEMENT OF ACE.

PRIORITY 3

3

TRANSPORTATION BY AIR OF CARGO, PERSONNEL, OR MAIL DETERMINED TO BE OF AN IMPORTANT NATURE IN ORDER TO ACCOMPLISH A MISSION. COVERS AIR TRAFFIC IN SUPPORT OF ALLIED FORCES BEING MAINTAINED IN A STATE OF READINESS FOR IMMEDIATE COMBAT OR DIRECT COMBAT SUPPORT, AND CASUALTY EVACUATION FROM A COMBAT ZONE.

PRIORITY 4

4

TRANSPORTATION BY AIR OF CARGO, PERSONNEL, OR MAIL DETERMINED TO BE NECESSARY, BUT WHICH DOES NOT MEET THE REQUIREMENTS FOR MOVEMENT AS SPECIFIED FOR PRIORITIES 1, 2, OR 3. COVERS AIR TRAFFIC IN SUPPORT OF THE REPAIR OF PRIMARY WEAPON SYSTEMS AND EQUIPMENT, AND ROUTINE RESUPPLY.
