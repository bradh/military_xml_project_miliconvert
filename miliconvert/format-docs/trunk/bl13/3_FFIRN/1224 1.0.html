Unit of Measure:

 

 

COMMUNICATIONS EVENT (Data Item)

Data Code

Explanation

ACTIVATION

ACTIVATION

TO PUT A COMMUNICATION FACILITY INTO EXISTENCE BY OFFICIAL ORDER SO IT CAN FUNCTION IN ITS ASSIGNED CAPACITY.

ALTERNATE

ALTERNATE

REFERS TO THE CHANGE OF LOCATION OF WHQS.

BACKLOG

BACKLOG

THE POINT AT WHICH THE COMMUNICATION SYSTEM(S) HAVE REACHED THEIR CAPACITY AND CAN NO LONGER PROCESS TRAFFIC AS IT IS GENERATED BY THE USERS OF THE SYSTEM(S).

DEACTIVATE

DEACTIVATE

PLACING A COMMUNICATIONS FACILITY IN AN INACTIVE STATUS.

DESTRUCTION

DESTRUCTION

THE PLACING OF COMMUNICATIONS FACILITIES/EQUIPMENT IN AN UNUSABLE AND IRREPARABLE CONDITION

DISRUPTION

DISRUPTION

INDICATES THE COMMUNICATION SERVICE NORMALLY PROVIDED BY A COMMUNICATION FACILITY HAS BEEN DISRUPTED BY SOME CAUSE.

JAMMING

JAMMING

INDICATES THE COMMUNICATION FACILITY SERVICE IS EXPERIENCING DIFFICULTY OR LOSS OF SERVICE DUE TO RADIATION, RERADIATION, OR REFLECTION OF ELECTROMAGNETIC ENERGY.

OTHER

OTHER

ANY EVENT OTHER THAN THE ABOVE SPECIFIC DATA ITEMS. ENTER EXPLANATION IN SUBSEQUENT FREE TEXT SET

OUTAGE

OUTAGE

INDICATES THE COMMUNICATION FACILITY/SERVICE HAS SUFFERED OR WILL SUFFER A LOSS OF SERVICE.

RECONFIGURATION

RECONFIG

INDICATES THE COMMUNICATION FACILITY/SERVICE HAS BEEN OR IS RECONFIGURED TO PROVIDE/DELETE A COMMUNICATION SERVICE.

RELOCATION

RELOCATION

INDICATES THE COMMUNICATION FACILITY/SERVICE HAS BEEN/WILL BE RELOCATED TO A NEW AREA.

RESTORATION

RESTORATION

INDICATES THE DISRUPTED COMMUNICATION SERVICE HAS BEEN RESTORED TO AN ACTIVE STATUS AFTER A TEMPORARY OUTAGE.
