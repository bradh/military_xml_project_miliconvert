Table 1376/1 - DOWNED AIRCRAFT CREW STATUS (3-4)

 

Related Documents:

None

 

Sponsor:

None

 

Unit of Measure:

 

 

DOWNED AIRCRAFT CREW STATUS (Data Item)

Data Code

Explanation

CAPTURED

CPTD

CREW MEMBER(S) WERE OBSERVED BEING TAKEN INTO CUSTODY BY THE ENEMY.

PARACHUTED

CHUT

CREW MEMBER(S) WERE OBSERVED PARACHUTING FROM THE PLANE PRIOR TO DESTRUCTION OR IMPACT. NUMBER OF PARACHUTES MAY BE REPORTED IN A FREE TEXT SET.

ASSUMED KILLED IN ACTION

AKIA

DUE TO THE NATURE OF THE DESTRUCTION OF THE AIRCRAFT, ALL CREWMEN WERE ASSUMED DEAD.

UNKNOWN

UNK

DISPOSITION OF AIRCRAFT CREW IS UNKNOWN.

RESCUED

RESC

CREW MEMBER(S) WERE RESCUED.

CAPTURE APPEARS IMMINENT

CPTI

CREW MEMBER(S) WERE OBSERVED IN OR NEAR THE VICINITY OF THE ENEMY.

SAFE FROM CAPTURE

SAFE

CREW MEMBER(S) OBSERVED NOT TO BE IN OR NEAR THE VICINITY OF THE ENEMY.

PROBABLE CAPTURED

CPTP

CAPTURE NOT CONFIRMED, BUT SITUATION SUCH THAT CREW MEMBER(S) WERE BELIEVED TO HAVE BEEN CAPTURED.

NO CHUTES

NOCH

AIRCRAFT CRASH OBSERVED, BUT NO CHUTE(S) SIGHTED.

KILLED IN ACTION

KIA

CREW MEMBER(S) HAVE BEEN CONFIRMED DEAD.

MISSING IN ACTION

MIA

LOCATION AND CONDITION OF CREW MEMBER(S) UNKNOWN.

EVADING

EVAD

CREW MEMBER(S) ARE ON THE GROUND AND EVADING THE ENEMY.

INJURED

INJR

CREW MEMBER(S) HAVE SUSTAINED INJURIES WHICH MAY BE A FACTOR IN THEIR SURVIVAL OR RESCUE.

AWAITING PICKUP

AWPU

DOWNED CREW MEMBER(S) ARE AWAITING SAR PICKUP.
