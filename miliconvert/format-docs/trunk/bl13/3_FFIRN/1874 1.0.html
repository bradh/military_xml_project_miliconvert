COMBAT CONTROL TEAM

CCT

(U.S.) A SMALL TASK ORGANIZED TEAM OF PARACHUTE AND COMBAT DIVER QUALIFIED PERSONNEL TRAINED AND EQUIPPED TO RAPIDLY ESTABLISH AND CONTROL DROP, LANDING, AND EXTRACTION ZONE AIR TRAFFIC IN AUSTERE OR HOSTILE CONDITIONS. IT PREPARES AND EXECUTES PROCEDURES FOR PEACETIME, CRISIS, PEACE SUPPORT, AND WAR OPERATIONS (AIRNORTH).
