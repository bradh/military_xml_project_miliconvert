ITEM AVAILABILITY (Data Item)

Data Code

Explanation

UNKNOWN

U

None

INCONCLUSIVE ANALYSIS

Z

None

INCAPABLE OF PRODUCING

X

UNDER CURRENT CONDITIONS PRODUCTION IS NOT VIABLE.

COLLOCATED

COLO

MATERIAL IS AVAILABLE AT THE SAME LOCATION.

CONSTRAINED

CONS

MATERIAL IS SCARCE, COSTLY, UNECONOMICAL OR FEW SUPPLIERS. USE OF MATERIAL MAY BE LIMITED AND MATERIAL MAY BE UNAVAILABLE AT TIMES.

LONG DISTANCE AVAILABILITY

LONG

MATERIAL IS AVAILABLE DUE TO TRADE AGREEMENTS, ECONOMIC TIES, OR OPEN MARKET ACCESS BUT IS DELIVERED OVER LONG DISTANCES BY TRAIN, TRUCK, BOAT OR PIPELINE. DELIVERY CAN BE NO SOONER THAN ONE WEEK AND IS USUALLY SEVERAL WEEKS.

PRODUCED

PROD

PRODUCT IS CURRENTLY BEING PRODUCED AND IS AVAILABLE.

PROJECTED

PROJ

NOT CURRENTLY AVAILABLE BUT PRODUCTION IS PLANNED FOR THE FUTURE.

READY TO PRODUCE

RDY

CAPABLE OF PRODUCING BUT NOT CURRENTLY PRODUCING PRODUCT.

SHORT DISTANCE AVAILABILITY

SHRT

MATERIAL IS AVAILABLE AND IS DELIVERED OVER SHORT DISTANCES BY TRAIN, TRUCK, OR PIPELINE. DELIVERY CAN BE NEXT DAY AND IS USUALLY LESS THAN A WEEK.
