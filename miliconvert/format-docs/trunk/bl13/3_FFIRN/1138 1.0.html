1138

 

Field identifier (Name):

1138 (COLOUR)

 

 

Definition:

THE COMMON NAMES OF THE SPECTRAL COLOURS. IN MILITARY USAGE, COLOUR NAMES MAY BE USED FOR SIGNALLING PURPOSES (MARKING PANELS, SMOKE, FLARES, ETC.), FOR DESIGNATING SPECIFIC ITEMS OF A GENERAL NATURE (RED BEACH, ROUTE GREEN, RED TEAM, ETC.), AS WELL AS FOR DESCRIBING THEPERCEIVED VISUAL CHARACTERISTIC OF COLOUR OF OBJECTS OR PHENOMENA.
