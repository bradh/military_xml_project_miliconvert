Field identifier (Name):

1374 (CAUSE OF AIRCRAFT LOSS)

 

 

Definition:

THE PROXIMATE CAUSE FOR THE LOSS OF SPECIFIC AIRCRAFT.

 

 

Sponsor:

None

 

 

Remarks:

LOSS MEANS THAT THE AIRCRAFT WAS EITHER PHYSICALLY DESTROYED, DAMAGED BEYOND CAPABILITY TO REPAIR OR REBUILD, OR LANDED IN AN AREA WHICH PRECLUDES RECOVERY DUE TO POLITICAL, MILITARY, OR GEOGRAPHICAL/ ENVIRONMENTAL CONSIDERATIONS.

 

 

Related Documents:         

MAS (AIR BOARD) USAF-76 84, DATED 24 JULY 1984

 

 

Status:  

In Service
