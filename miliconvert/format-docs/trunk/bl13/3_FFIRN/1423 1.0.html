Data Code

Explanation

STABLE AND SMOOTH FLAT BOTTOM

A

RIPPLES LESS THAN 15 CM DEEP AND/OR MODERATE MINE BURIAL POSSIBLE BUT NEVER EXCEEDING 15 CM.

RATHER STABLE AND SMOOTH BUT UNEVEN BOTTOM

B

HOLES, BUMPS, RIDGES AND FOLDS UP TO 30 CM AND/OR MINE BURIAL POSSIBLE BUT NEVER EXCEEDING 30 CM

ROUGH BOTTOM

C

HOLES, BUMPS, RIDGES AND FOLDS UP TO 30 CM. AND/OR A LOT OF SEAWEED. MINE BURIAL LIKELY (EXCEEDING 30 CM) BUT NEVER COMPLETE.

MINES ARE LIKELY TO BE HIDDEN COMPLETELY OWING TO COMPLETE BURIAL

DB

MINES MAY BE BURIED PERMANENTLY OR BREAK SURFACE FROM TIME TO TIME.
