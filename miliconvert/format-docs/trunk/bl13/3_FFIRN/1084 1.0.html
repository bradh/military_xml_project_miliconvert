RESIDUAL MISSION INDICATOR

1-1

Individual

AN INDICATOR OF WHETHER THE MISSION IS OR WILL BE A RESIDUAL MISSION.  MISSIONS THAT SPAN MORE THAN ONE TASKING PERIOD WILL REAPPEAR IN EACH APPLICABLE MESSAGE FOR COORDINATION AND FLIGHT SAFETY PURPOSES.  THE FIRST TIME THIS TYPE OF MISSION IS PUBLISHED, IT IS MARKED AS "FORWARDED."  IN SUBSEQUENT APPLICABLE MESSAGES, THE MISSION LINES ARE COPIED VERBATIM AND MARKED AS "RESIDUAL."  MISSIONS THAT DO NOT SPAN MULTIPLE TASKING PERIODS ARE MARKED "NON-RESIDUAL."
