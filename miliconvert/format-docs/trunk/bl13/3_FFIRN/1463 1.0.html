SURFACE SHIP HULL PROFILE, BASED UPON THE PATTERN OF RAISED SEGMENTS OF HULL PROFILE.  MEANING OF 1, 2, AND 3 : 1 = BOW 2 = AMIDSHIPS 3 = STERN.
