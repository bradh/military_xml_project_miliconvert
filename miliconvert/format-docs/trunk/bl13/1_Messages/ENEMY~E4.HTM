ENEMY CONTACT REP

 

Message identifier (Name):

ENEMY CONTACT REP (ENEMY CONTACT REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 5621

 

 

Purpose:

THE ENEMY CONTACT REP IS USED TO REPORT THE RESULTS OF CONTACT WITH THE ENEMY AT THE CORPS LEVEL AND BELOW.

 

 

Sponsor:

SHAPE

 

 

Notes:

None

None

 

 

Status:
