NBC EDR

 

Message identifier (Name):

NBC EDR (NBC EFFECTIVE DOWNWIND REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

AEP-45
ATP-45
STANAG 2103
STANAG 2497

 

 

Purpose:

THE NBC EDR IS USED TO PROVIDE THE EFFECTIVE DOWNWIND DATA NEEDED FOR PREDICTION OF FALLOUT AREAS FOLLOWING NUCLEAR BURST FOR A TIME PERIOD OF AT LEAST 6 HOURS SPECIFIED IN THE MESSAGE, INCLUDING SPECIFIC DOWNWIND SPEEDS AND DIRECTIONS FOR UP TO SEVEN SELECTED WEAPON YIELDS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) NUCLEAR, BIOLOGICAL AND CHEMICAL DEFENSE OPERATIONS INTERSERVICE WORKING GROUP (NBCWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
