BARNSTORM

 

Message identifier (Name):

BARNSTORM (BARNSTORM)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-18

 

 

Purpose:

THE BARNSTORM MESSAGE IS THE PRIMARY METHOD BY WHICH A SUBMARINE OPERATIONS AUTHORITY WILL PASS ALL MAJOR OPERATIONAL CONTROL, SAFETY AND COMMUNICATION INSTRUCTIONS TO SUBMARINES ON OPERATIONAL PATROLS AND DURING MAJOR EXERCISES READING A SUBMARINE BROADCAST. THUS IT IS POSSIBLE FOR THE OPERATIONAL COMMANDER WHO HOLDS A FULL SET OF BARNSTORM SIGNALS, TO BE CERTAIN THAT HE HAS A COMPLETE RECORD OF ALL OPERATIONAL SIGNALS SENT TO THAT SUBMARINE.

 

 

Sponsor:

MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPSWG)

 

 

Notes:

1.  The PASSAGE and MOVAREA sets should be used in combination to describe a passage plan with holding areas. For example, a passage through points AA, BB, CC, operate in an area called HOLDING AREA ALPHA and then return through points CC, BB, AA.
