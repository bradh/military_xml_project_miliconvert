Message identifier (Name):

RBTMAINTREP (ROAD, BRIDGE OR TUNNEL MAINTENANCE REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2430 (AENGRP-2 (A))

 

 

Purpose:

THE RBTMAINTREP IS USED TO REPORT THE RESULTS OF A SPECIFIC REPAIR OR MAINTENANCE TASK ALONG A SECTION OF A ROUTE.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) COMBAT ENGINEER WORKING GROUP (ENGRWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
