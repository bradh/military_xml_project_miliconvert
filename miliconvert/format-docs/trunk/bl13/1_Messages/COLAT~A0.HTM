COLATDAMREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

COLATDAMREP (COLLATERAL DAMAGE REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE COLATDAMREP IS USED TO REPORT COLLATERAL DAMAGE, IN SINGLE OR MULTIPLE INCIDENTS, TO SUBORDINATE, ADJACENT AND HIGHER UNITS/FORMATIONS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (LOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

COLLATERAL DAMAGE IS THE RESULT OF ANY ACTION WHICH CAUSES UNWANTED DEATH OR INJURY TO NEUTRAL PERSONNEL OR DAMAGE TO THEIR PROPERTY. IT MAY BE UNINTENTIONAL OR IT MAY BE THE RESULT OF A CALCULATED RISK.

 

 

Status:

In Service
