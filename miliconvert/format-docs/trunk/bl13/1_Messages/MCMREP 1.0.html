Message identifier (Name):

MCMREP (MINE COUNTERMEASURES REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-24
ATP-6
CNBA SOP 15

 

 

Purpose:

THE MCMREP IS USED TO PROVIDE A SITUATION REPORT ON MINE COUNTERMEASURES OPERATIONS AND THE AREAS IMPACTED BY PRESENT OR FUTURE MINE COUNTERMEASURES OPERATIONS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) NAVAL MINE WARFARE WORKING GROUP (NMWWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
