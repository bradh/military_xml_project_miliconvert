Message identifier (Name):

CROSSDECKRESP (CROSS-DECK OPERATIONS RESPONSE)

 

 

Related Documents:

APP-2
STANAG 3117
STANAG 3779

 

 

Purpose:

THE CROSSDECKRESP IS USED TO PROVIDE A RESPONSE WITH RESPECT TO NATO CROSS-DECK OPERATIONS TO TRANSFER CAPABLE AIRCRAFT TO OR FROM A MARITIME PLATFORM.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) HELICOPTER OPERATIONS FROM SHIPS OTHER THAN AIRCRAFT CARRIER TACTICAL WORKING GROUP (HOST

 

 

Notes:

None
