CISUPINTREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

CISUPINTREP (COUNTER-INTELLIGENCE AND SECURITY SUPPLEMENTARY REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2511

 

 

Purpose:

THE CISUPINTREP IS USED TO PROVIDE ALL ADDRESSEES WITH A COMPREHENSIVE REVIEW OF ALL COUNTER-INTELLIGENCE (CI) DATA COLLECTED OVER AN EXTENDED PERIOD OF TIME INCLUDING AN ASSESSMENT OF TRENDS IN THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE CI SITUATION. THE CISUPINTREP IS ALSO USED TO PROVIDE A COMPREHENSIVE REVIEW OF ONE OR SEVERAL SPECIFIC CI PROJECTS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (JSB) JOINT INTELLIGENCE WORKING GROUP (JINTWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
