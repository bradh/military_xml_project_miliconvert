ENGGSRECCEORD

 

Message identifier (Name):

ENGGSRECCEORD (GENERAL ENGINEER SUPPORT RECONNAISSANCE ORDER)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2430 (AENGRP-2 (A))

 

 

Purpose:

THE ENGGSRECCEORD IS USED TO ORDER A RECONNAISSANCE FOR GENERAL ENGINEER SUPPORT TASKS OR THOSE TASKS WHICH DO NOT FALL UNDER THE CATEGORIES OF MOBILITY, COUNTER-MOBILITY AND SURVIVABILITY.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) COMBAT ENGINEER WORKING GROUP (ENGRWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
