LOGASSESSREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

LOGASSESSREP (LOGISTIC ASSESSMENT REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2014
STANAG 2406 (ALP-9)

 

 

Purpose:

THE LOGASSESSREP IS USED TO STANDARDISE THE METHOD FOR INFORMING SUPERIOR HEADQUARTERS OF THE COMMAND'S LOGISTICS STATUS AND TO PROVIDE AN ASSESSMENT OF THE OVERALL LOGISTICS SITUATION FOR FORCES, TOGETHER WITH INTENDED OR RECOMMENDED ACTION.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND FORCES LOGISTICS DOCTRINE WORKING GROUP (LOGWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
