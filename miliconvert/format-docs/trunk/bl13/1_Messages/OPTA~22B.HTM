OPTASK AIR

 

Message identifier (Name):

OPTASK AIR (OPERATIONAL TASKING ORGANIC AIRCRAFT)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1 VOL I
AXP-5

 

 

Purpose:

THE OPTASK AIR IS USED FOR THE OTC OR DELEGATED AUTHORITY TO PROMULGATE DETAILED TASKING AND INSTRUCTIONS FOR ALL ORGANIC AIRCRAFT. THIS MESSAGE IS NORMALLY PROMULGATED BY THE OTC OR THE AIR COORDINATOR.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
