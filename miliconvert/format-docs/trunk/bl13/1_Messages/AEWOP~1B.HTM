AEW OPSTAT

 

Message identifier (Name):

AEW OPSTAT (AIRBORNE EARLY WARNING STATUS OF FORCES REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

NONE

 

 

Purpose:

THE AEW OPSTAT IS USED TO PROVIDE THE OPERATIONAL STATUS OF AEW FORCES, AS WELL AS A SUMMARY OF EVENTS OVER THE PREVIOUS 24 HOURS.

 

 

Sponsor:

BI-MNC OPERATIONS REPORTS WORKING GROUP (ORWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
