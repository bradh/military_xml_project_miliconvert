ATO (AIR TASKING ORDER)

 

 

Related Documents:

AJP-1   Allied Joint Doctrine
AJP-3   Allied Joint Operations
AJP-3.3 Joint Air   Space Operations Doctrine

 

 

Purpose:

THE ATO IS USED TO TASK AIR, SURFACE BASED AIR DEFENSE AND SURFACE BASED AIR SURVEILLANCE AND CONTROL ASSETS IN ORDER TO CONDUCT BOTH JOINT OR SINGLE SERVICE AIR OPERATIONS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (AIR) AIR OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (AOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

THE ATO CAN BE USED FOR BOTH PRE-PLANNED AND IMMEDIATE TASKING.

 

 

Status:

In Service
