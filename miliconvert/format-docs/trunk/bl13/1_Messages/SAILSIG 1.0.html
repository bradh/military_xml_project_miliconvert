SAILSIG

 

Message identifier (Name):

SAILSIG (SAILING SIGNAL - LOAD ADVICE)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2166
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE SAILSIG IS USED TO PROVIDE INFORMATION TO LAND FORCES LOGISTICIANS ON SHIP SAILING AND DETAILS ON CARGO. IT ADVISES AUTHORITIES AT THE DESTINATION PORT OF AN ACTUAL SAILING FROM THE DEPARTURE PORT WHERE THE TRANSIT TIME IS LESS THAN 96 HOURS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) MOVEMENT AND TRANSPORT WORKING GROUP (M T WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
