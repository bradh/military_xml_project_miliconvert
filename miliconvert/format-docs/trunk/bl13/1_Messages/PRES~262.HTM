PRESENCE

 

Message identifier (Name):

PRESENCE (PRESENCE REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE PRESENCE REPORT IS USED TO INFORM A COMMANDER ON THE DEPLOYMENT OF MILITARY ORGANIZATIONS WITHIN HIS AREA OF RESPONSIBILITY. THE REPORT ADDRESSES BOTH ORGANIZATIONS UNDER HIS COMMAND AND THOSE THAT ARE NOT.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (LOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
