OPTASK AAW

 

Message identifier (Name):

OPTASK AAW (OPERATIONAL TASKING ANTI-AIR WARFARE)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1

 

 

Purpose:

THE OPTASK AAW IS USED TO PROMULGATE DETAILED TASKING AND INSTRUCTIONS FOR ALL ASPECTS OF ANTI-AIR WARFARE AND PROVIDE SAFETY AND COORDINATION INFORMATION FOR SHORE BASED AIRCRAFT JOINING THE FORCE AT SEA.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
