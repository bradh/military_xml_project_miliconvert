PWPERSREC

 

Message identifier (Name):

PWPERSREC (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL RECORD)

 

 

Related Documents:

AINTP-3
STANAG 1059
STANAG 2044
STANAG 2195 (AJP-2.5)

 

 

Purpose:

THE PWPERSREC IS USED TO RECORD AND GIVE INFORMATION ON THE PERSONAL DETAILS OF A PRISONER OF WAR, IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE RULES OF THE GENEVA CONVENTION.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (JSB) JOINT INTELLIGENCE WORKING GROUP (JINTWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service

SPECIFIES PERSONAL RELATIONSHIP DATA OF AHE PRISONER OF WAR.

THIS SEGMENT CONTAINS SETS "PERSONRS" AND "POSTADDR".  TWO OCCURRENCES OF THE SEGMENT ARE MANDATORY: FIRST, PERSON TO BE INFORMED ABOUT THE CAPTURE ("TBI" MUST BE USED IN FIELD 1 OF FIRST OCCANCE) AND SECONDLY, NEXT OF KIN ("NOK" MUST BE USED IN FIELD ONE OF SECOND OCCURANCE).

 

SEGMENT 18 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL RELATION DATA SEGMENT) IN EVERY OCCURRENCE OF SEGMENT 12 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL INFORMATION SEGMENT) IN EVERY OCCURRENCE OF SEGMENT 7 (PRISONER OF WAR INFORMATION SEGMENT) MUST OCCUR MORE THAN 1 TIMES.

PERSONRS

18

PERSONAL RELATIONSHIP DATA

IDENTIFIES AN INDIVIDUAL TO WHOM THE PRISONER OF WAR HAS A SPECIFIC PERSONAL RELATIONSHIP. 

FIELD 1 IN SET 18 (PERSONRS) IN OCCURRENCE 1 OF SEGMENT 18 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL RELATION DATA SEGMENT) IN EVERY OCCURRENCE OF SEGMENT 12 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL INFORMATION SEGMENT) IN EVERY OCCURRENCE OF SEGMENT 7 (PRISONER OF WAR INFORMATION SEGMENT) MUST EQUAL /TBI/.

FIELD 2 IN SET 19 (POSTADDR) IN SEGMENT 18 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL RELATION DATA SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 12 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL INFORMATION SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 7 (PRISONER OF WAR INFORMATION SEGMENT) IS REQUIRED IF FIELD 1 IN SET 19 (POSTADDR) IN SEGMENT 18 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL RELATION DATA SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 12 (PRISONER OF WAR PERSONAL INFORMATION SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 7 (PRISONER OF WAR INFORMATION SEGMENT) DOES NOT OCCUR.
