MOVASSESSREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

MOVASSESSREP (MOVEMENT ASSESSMENT REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SCD 80-3
STANAG 2014
STANAG 2135
STANAG 2406

 

 

Purpose:

THE MOVASSESSREP IS USED TO PROVIDE NATO COMMANDERS AND NATIONS AN ASSESSMENT OF HOST NATION AND/OR THEATRE MOVEMENT & TRANSPORT (M&T) RESOURCES AND THE RELATED INFRA-STRUCTURE IN SUPPORT OF AN OPERATION, AND TO REPORT CURRENT AND/OR RECOMMENDED REMEDIAL ACTION.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) MOVEMENT AND TRANSPORT WORKING GROUP (M T WG). SHAPE

 

 

Notes:

ALL ASSESSMENT SETS (10 - 17) WILL PROVIDE AN OVERALL ASSESSMENT OF THEIR RESPECTIVE LINES OF COMMUNICATONS AREA AND THE RELATED INFRASTRUCTURE, PARTICULARLY WHERE THEY HAVE IMPACT ON REDUCTION IN CAPACITY OR EFFICIENCY OF MOVEMENT NETWORKS, INSTALLATIONS AND FACILITIES.  REPORT PROBLEMS, CONCERNS AND EQUIPMENT OR RESOURCE SHORTAGES HAVING ADVERSE IMPACT ON THE RESPECTIVE AREA.  ALSO INCLUDE ON-GOING/RECOMMENDED REMEDIAL ACTIONS WHERE APPROPRIATE.  ENTER "NO CHANGE" IN ANY AREA WHERE NO REMARKS ARE MADE.

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
