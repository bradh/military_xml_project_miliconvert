STRIKWARN

 

Message identifier (Name):

STRIKWARN (NUCLEAR STRIKE WARNING MESSAGE)

 

 

Related Documents:

AEP-45
ATP-45
STANAG 2103
STANAG 2104
STANAG 2111
STANAG 2497

 

 

Purpose:

THE STRIKWARN IS USED TO PROVIDE FRIENDLY FORCES WITH THE NECESSARY INFORMATION NEEDED TO TAKE SAFETY PRECAUTIONS AGAINST A FRIENDLY NUCLEAR BURST.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) NUCLEAR, BIOLOGICAL AND CHEMICAL DEFENSE OPERATIONS INTERSERVICE WORKING GROUP (NBCWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
