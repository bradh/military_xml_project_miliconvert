CASEVACREQ

 

Message identifier (Name):

CASEVACREQ (CASUALTY EVACUATION REQUEST)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2087
STANAG 2434
STANAG 2999
STANAG 3204

 

 

Purpose:

THE CASEVACREQ IS USED TO REQUEST MEDICAL CASUALTY EVACUATION SUPPORT FOR SINGLE AND MULTIPLE EVACUATION AND BY WHATEVER MEANS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (LOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service

GNDSIGRC

12

GROUND SIGNALS AND RECOGNITION

DEFINES THE SIGNAL DEVICE TO BE USED TO ENSURE THAT MUTUAL RECOGNITION OF CASUALTIES IN AN OPERATION.  ALSO ADDITIONALLY IDENTIFIES THE SHAPE IN WHICH A GROUND MARKING PANEL IS POSITIONED.

FIELD 2 IN SET 12 (GNDSIGRC) IN SEGMENT 11 (CASUALTY EVACUATION REQUIREMENT SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 9 (EVACUATION SEGMENT) IS REQUIRED IF FIELD 1 IN SET 12 (GNDSIGRC) IN SEGMENT 11 (CASUALTY EVACUATION REQUIREMENT SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 9 (EVACUATION SEGMENT) EQUALS /SMOKE/ OR /PANELS/ OR /STROBE BEACON/ OR /STAR CLUSTER/ OR /LIGHTS/ OR /TORCH/.

FIELD 3 IN SET 12 (GNDSIGRC) IN SEGMENT 11 (CASUALTY EVACUATION REQUIREMENT SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 9 (EVACUATION SEGMENT) IS REQUIRED IF FIELD 1 IN SET 12 (GNDSIGRC) IN SEGMENT 11 (CASUALTY EVACUATION REQUIREMENT SEGMENT) IN SEGMENT 9 (EVACUATION SEGMENT) EQUALS /PANELS/, OTHERWISE IT IS PROHIBITED.
