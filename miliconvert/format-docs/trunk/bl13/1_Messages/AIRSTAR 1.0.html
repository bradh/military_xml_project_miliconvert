AIRSTAR

 

Message identifier (Name):

AIRSTAR (AIR STATUS REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE VOL III ACE AIR SUPPLEMENT

 

 

Purpose:

THE AIRSTAR IS USED TO REPORT THE OPERATIONAL STATUS OF AIR BASES AND TO PROVIDE ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ON AIR BASE RESOURCES.

 

 

Sponsor:

(MC ASB) AIR OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (AOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
