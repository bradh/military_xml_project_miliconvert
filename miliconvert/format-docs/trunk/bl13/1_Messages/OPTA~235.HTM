OPTASK COMMS

 

Message identifier (Name):

OPTASK COMMS (OPERATIONAL TASKING COMMUNICATIONS)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1

 

 

Purpose:

THE OPTASK COMMS IS USED TO PROMULGATE THE COMMUNICATIONS PLAN IN FORCE AND PROVIDE COMMUNICATIONS RELATED INSTRUCTIONS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
