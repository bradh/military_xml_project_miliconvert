CIINTSUM

 

Message identifier (Name):

CIINTSUM (COUNTER-INTELLIGENCE AND SECURITY SUMMARY)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2511
STANAG 2844

 

 

Purpose:

THE CIINTSUM IS USED TO INFORM ADDRESSEES PERIODICALLY ON CURRENT COUNTER-INTELLIGENCE AND SECURITY AND TO PROVIDE ESTIMATE OF THREAT POSED BY HOSTILE INTELLIGENCE SERVICES (HIS) OR SUBVERSIVE GROUPS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (JSB) JOINT INTELLIGENCE WORKING GROUP (JINTWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
