PCS INTENTIONS

 

Message identifier (Name):

PCS INTENTIONS (PRIMARY CONTROL SHIP INTENTIONS)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1
ATP-36
ATP-39
ATP-8

 

 

Purpose:

THE PCS INTENTIONS IS USED TO PROVIDE DETAILED INSTRUCTIONS TO AMPHIBIOUS UNITS CONCERNING THE CONDUCT OF THE SURFACE ASSAULT PORTION OF AN AMPHIBIOUS OPERATION.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) AMPHIBIOUS OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (AMPHIB OPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
