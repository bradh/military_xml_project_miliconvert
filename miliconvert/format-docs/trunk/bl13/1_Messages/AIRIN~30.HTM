AIRINTREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

AIRINTREP (AIR INTELLIGENCE REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE, VOL. II

 

 

Purpose:

THE AIRINTREP IS USED TO INFORM SACEUR AND ACO COMMANDERS OF CHANGES IN THE LOCATION, DISPOSITION, STATUS AND OTHER ESSENTIAL ELEMENTS OF INFORMATION CONCERNING NON-NATO AIR ORDER OF BATTLE.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (JSB) JOINT INTELLIGENCE WORKING GROUP (JINTWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
