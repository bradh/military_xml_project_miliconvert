Message identifier (Name):

AARCTM (AIR TO AIR REFUELLING COMBINED TASK MESSAGE)

 

 

Related Documents:

AJP-3.3

 

 

Purpose:

THE AARCTM IS USED TO TASK TANKER MISSIONS AND THOSE MISSIONS SUPPORTED BY AIR TO AIR REFUELLING.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (AIR) AIR OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (AOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
