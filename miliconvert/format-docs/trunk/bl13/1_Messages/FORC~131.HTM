FORCEPREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

FORCEPREP (FORCE PREPARE MESSAGE)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE, VOL. III

 

 

Purpose:

THE FORCEPREP IS USED TO PROVIDE A NATIONAL RESPONSE TO MNC WARNING MESSAGES. THIS MESSAGE IS A STATEMENT OF A NATION'S INTENTION TO PROVIDE FORCES REQUESTED BY AN MNC WARNING MESSAGE OR ACTIVATION REQUEST.

 

 

Sponsor:

BI-SC OPERATIONS REPORTS WORKING GROUP (ORWG).

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
