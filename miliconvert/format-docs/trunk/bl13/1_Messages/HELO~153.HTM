HELOPSUM

 

Message identifier (Name):

HELOPSUM (HELICOPTER OPERATIONAL SUMMARY)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE
STANAG 2434
STANAG 2999

 

 

Purpose:

THE HELOPSUM IS USED TO STANDARDISE THE METHOD FOR SUBMITTING DAILY HELICOPTER OPERATIONAL SUMMARY REPORTS, AT A TIME DEFINED BY THE APPROPRIATE AUTHORITY.

 

 

Sponsor:

SHAPE

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
