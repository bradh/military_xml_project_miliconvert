RMPSITSUM

 

Message identifier (Name):

RMPSITSUM (RMP SITUATION SUMMARY)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE 80-3

 

 

Purpose:

THE RMPSITSUM IS USED TO PERIODICALLY REPORT THE LOCATION OF  SURFACE AND/OR SUB-SURFACE CONTACTS AND/OR THE OVERLAY DETAILS OF A SPECIFIED GEOGRAPHICAL AREA, COMPRISING THE RECOGNIZED MARITIME PICTURE (RMP).

 

 

Sponsor:

BI-SC  IOECMB

 

 

Notes:

RMPSITSUM ENABLES THE TRANSFER OF THE NATO RECOGNIZED MARITIME PICTURE (RMP) INFORMATION TO MCCIS OR OTHER C3 SYSTEMS THAT ARE NOT CONNECTED TO NIDTS/CRONOS.

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
