ORBATTOASEA

 

Message identifier (Name):

ORBATTOASEA (ORDER OF BATTLE TRANSFER OF AUTHORITY SEA)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE, VOL. III

 

 

Purpose:

THE ORBATTOASEA IS USED TO TRANSFER OPERATIONAL COMMAND AND/OR CONTROL FROM ONE AUTHORITY TO ANOTHER.

 

 

Sponsor:

BI-SC OPERATIONS REPORTS WORKING GROUP (ORWG).

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:
