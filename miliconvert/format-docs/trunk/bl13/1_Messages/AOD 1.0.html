Message identifier (Name):

AOD (AIR OPERATIONS DIRECTIVE)

 

 

Related Documents:

AJP-3.3

 

 

Purpose:

THE AOD IS USED TO PROVIDE DIRECTIVES TO COMBINED AIR OPERATIONS CENTRE (CAOC) COMMANDER (COMCAOC) FOR THE EMPLOYMENT OF AIR ASSETS AND INCLUDES AIR DEFENCE (AD) PRIORITIES, AD POSTURES, TASK FORCES ALLOCATIONS AND MISSION ORDERS

 

 

Sponsor:

(MC ASB) AIR OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (AOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
