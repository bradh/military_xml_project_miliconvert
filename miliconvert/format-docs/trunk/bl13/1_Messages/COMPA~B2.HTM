COMPASSESSREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

COMPASSESSREP (COMPLIANCE ASSESSMENT REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE COMPASSESSREP IS USED TO PROVIDE SCS AND NATO HQ INFORMATION OF THE PARTIES' COMPLIANCE WITH ACCEPTED AGREEMENTS CONCERNING THE DESIGNATED 'SAFE' OR OTHER AREA(S)/EXCLUSION ZONE(S)/SEPARATION ZONE(S). THIS REPORT MAY INCLUDE ASSESSMENTS.

 

 

Sponsor:

SHAPE

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
