TRANSAR

 

Message identifier (Name):

TRANSAR (NATO AIR TRANSPORT ACCEPT REFUSE REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-53

 

 

Purpose:

THE TRANSAR IS USED BY NATO MILITARY AIRLIFT AGENCIES TO CONFIRM TO ALL PARTIES THAT A NATION HAS ACCEPTED TASKING FOR THE OPERATION OF AN AIRLIFT MISSION THROUGH NATO COORDINATION. IN THIS REGARD, IT IS A FORMAL TASKING TO THE PROVIDING NATION(S) AND A FORMAL REPLY TO THE REQUESTING NATION. THIS FORMAT IS ALSO USED FOR REFUSAL OF AN AIR TRANSPORT REQUEST.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (AIR) AIR TRANSPORT WORKING GROUP (TN WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
