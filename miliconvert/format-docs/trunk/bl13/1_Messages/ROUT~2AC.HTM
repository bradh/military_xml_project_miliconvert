Message identifier (Name):

ROUTERECCEREP (ROUTE RECONNAISSANCE REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2430 (AENGRP-2 (A))

 

 

Purpose:

THE ROUTERECCEREP IS USED TO REPORT THE RESULTS OF A ROUTE RECONNAISSANCE AT BATTALION LEVEL AND BELOW.
