MLAYREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

MLAYREP (MINELAYING REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-24
ATP-6
CNBA SOP 15

 

 

Purpose:

THE MLAYREP IS USED TO REPORT THE STATUS OF MINELAYING OPERATIONS ON AN AREA BY AREA BASIS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) NAVAL MINE WARFARE WORKING GROUP (NMWWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
