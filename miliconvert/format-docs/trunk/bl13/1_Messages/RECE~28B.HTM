RECEXREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

RECEXREP (RECONNAISSANCE EXPLOITATION REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 3377
STANAG 3596

 

 

Purpose:

THE RECEXREP IS USED TO REPORT THE RESULTS OF AN AIR RECONNAISSANCE MISSION BY THE INTERPRETATION OF SENSOR DATA.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (AIR) AIR RECONNAISSANCE WORKING GROUP (ARWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
