NBC CDR

 

Message identifier (Name):

NBC CDR (NBC CHEMICAL (BIOLOGICAL) DOWNWIND REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

AEP-45
ATP-45
STANAG 2103
STANAG 2497

 

 

Purpose:

THE NBC CDR IS USED TO DISSEMINATE A FORECAST OF THE METEOROLOGICAL DATA NEEDED FOR THE CHEMICAL OR BIOLOGICAL HAZARD AREA PREDICTION PROCEDURE BY APPROPRIATE AGENCIES EVERY SIX HOURS FOR 3 CONSECUTIVE 2 HOUR PERIODS OR FOR A TIME PERIOD OF AT LEAST 6 HOURS SPECIFIED IN THE MESSAGE.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) NUCLEAR, BIOLOGICAL AND CHEMICAL DEFENSE OPERATIONS INTERSERVICE WORKING GROUP (NBCWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
