ATCORD

 

Message identifier (Name):

ATCORD (NATO AIRLIFT MISSION COORDINATION MESSAGE)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-53

 

 

Purpose:

THE ATCORD IS USED TO PROVIDE A COMMON FORMAT FOR COORDINATION OF NATO AIRLIFT TASKINGS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (AIR) AIR TRANSPORT WORKING GROUP (TN WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
