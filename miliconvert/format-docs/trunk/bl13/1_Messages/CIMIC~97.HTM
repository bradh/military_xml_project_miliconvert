CIMICREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

CIMICREP (CIVIL MILITARY COOPERATION REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE, VOL. III
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE CIMICREP IS USED TO KEEP COMMANDERS CONTINUOUSLY INFORMED DURING TIMES OF TENSION, CRISIS, PEACE SUPPORT OPERATIONS AND WAR OF MATTERS ASSOCIATED WITH THE COORDINATION OF CIVIL-MILITARY COOPERATION (AS PART OF CIVIL-MILITARY OPERATIONS) WHICH AFFECT, OR COULD AFFECT, THE CAPABILITIES AND/OR THE OPERATIONS OF FORCES UNDER THEIR COMMAND.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (LOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
