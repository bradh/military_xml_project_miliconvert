ICTYREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

ICTYREP (INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL TRIBUNAL FOR FORMER YUGOSLAVIA REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

REPORTING SYSTEM SUPPLEMENT FOR SACEURS OPLAN 10407 (JOINT FORGE)

 

 

Purpose:

THE ICTYREP IS USED TO PROVIDE SHAPE INFORMATION AND PEACE SUPPORT OPERATION FORCE (PSOF) COMMANDER'S ASSESSMENT OF THE PSOF ACTIVITIES IN SUPPORT OF THE INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL TRIBUNAL FOR FORMER YUGOSLAVIA.

 

 

Sponsor:

SHAPE OPSLOG OJW

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
