PRETECHREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

PRETECHREP (PRELIMINARY TECHNICAL REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2044
STANAG 2084
STANAG 2195 (AJP-2.5)
STANAG 2511

 

 

Purpose:

THE PRETECHREP IS USED TO REPORT THE ACQUISITION OF EQUIPMENT WHICH HAS INTELLIGENCE SIGNIFICANCE.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (JSB) JOINT INTELLIGENCE WORKING GROUP (JINTWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

THE REPORT IS TO BE SUBMITTED BY ACCELERATED INTELLIGENCE REPORTING PROCEDURES IMMEDIATELY FOLLOWING THE ACQUISITION OF SIGNIFICANT EQUIPMENT.

 

 

Status:

In Service
