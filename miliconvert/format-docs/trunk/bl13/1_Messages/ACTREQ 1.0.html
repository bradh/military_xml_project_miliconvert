ACTREQ

 

Message identifier (Name):

ACTREQ (ACTIVATION REQUEST MESSAGE)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE ACTREQ IS USED TO REQUEST AUTHORITY FROM THE NATO MILITARY COMMITTEE (NAMILCOM) TO ACTIVATE CONTINGENCY PLANS, ON CALL FORCES, SPECIAL SURVEILLANCE MISSIONS OR OTHER UNIQUE REQUIREMENT TO EMPLOY MILITARY FORCES.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (LOWG) SHAPE

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
