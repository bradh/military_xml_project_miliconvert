PERSREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

PERSREP (PERSONNEL REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE PERSREP IS USED TO PROVIDE COMMANDERS AND STAFFS WITH A SUMMARY OF PERSONNEL INFORMATION BY QUANTITIES AND CATEGORIES.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (LOWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
