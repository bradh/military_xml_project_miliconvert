OPSTAT CASP

 

Message identifier (Name):

OPSTAT CASP (OPERATIONAL STATUS CASP)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-3.3.3.1

 

 

Purpose:

THE OPSTAT CASP PROVIDES A STRUCTURE FOR COORDINATING AD OR AAW OPERATIONS WHEN AN AD CAPABLE WARSHIP IS OPERATING IN, OR ADJACENT TO, A JOINT OPERATIONS AREA (JOA). A WARSHIP MAY OPERATE UNDER ONE OF FOUR CASP CATEGORIES. THIS PERMITS IT TO CONTRIBUTE, TO VARYING DEGREES, TO THE AD BATTLE AND TO COORDINATE THEIR AAW OPERATIONS WITH LAND-BASED AIRCRAFT OR WEAPONS SYSTEMS.

 

 

Sponsor:

MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPSWG)

 

 

Notes:

1.  THE PASSAGE AND MOVAREA SETS SHOULD BE USED IN COMBINATION TO DESCRIBE A PASSAGE PLAN WITH HOLDING AREAS. FOR EXAMPLE, A PASSAGE THROUGH POINTS AA, BB, CC, OPERATE IN AN AREA CALLED HOLDING AREA ALPHA AND THEN RETURN THROUGH POINTS CC, BB, AA.
