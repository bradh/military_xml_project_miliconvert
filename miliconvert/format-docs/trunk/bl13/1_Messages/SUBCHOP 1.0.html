SUBCHOP

 

Message identifier (Name):

SUBCHOP (SUBMARINE CHANGE OF OPCON MESSAGE)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1 VOL I (MARITIME TACTICAL INSTRUCTIONS AND PROCEDURES)

 

 

Purpose:

THE SUBCHOP MESSAGE IS USED TO PROVIDE SUBOPAUTHS AND OTHER AFFECTED COMMANDERS WITH ALL INFORMATION RELEVANT TO CHANGE OF OPERATIONAL CONTROL (OPCON) OF SUBMARINES BEING CHOPPED.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
