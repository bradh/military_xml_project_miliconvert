Message identifier (Name):

PURPLE (MARITIME MISSION ANALYSIS SUMMARY REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1 VOL I

 

 

Purpose:

THE PURPLE IS USED TO PROVIDE A COMPREHENSIVE SUMMARY OF THE ACTIVITIES OF A MISSION OR EVENT.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
