CARGOTRAFFIC

 

Message identifier (Name):

CARGOTRAFFIC (CARGO TRAFFIC MESSAGE)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2166
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE CARGOTRAFFIC IS USED TO ADVISE LAND FORCES LOGISTICIANS, AT EACH PORT OF DISCHARGE, THE DETAILS OF THE CARGO TO BE UNLOADED AT THAT LOCATION SO THAT LOADING AND OFF-LOADING OPERATIONS MAY BE CARRIED OUT EFFICIENTLY.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) MOVEMENT AND TRANSPORT WORKING GROUP (M T WG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
