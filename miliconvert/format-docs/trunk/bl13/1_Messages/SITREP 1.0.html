SITREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

SITREP (SITUATION REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE SITREP IS USED TO PROVIDE SACEUR WITH INFORMATION OF THE COMMITTED FORCES CAPABILITIES WITH REGARD TO CURRENT AND RELEASE OPERATIONS AND THE OVERALL SITUATION OF THE INVOLVED PARTIES.

 

 

Sponsor:

SHAPE

 

 

Notes:

ADDITIONAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR SET 12, LAND OPERATIONS.  INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING ITEMS:A. SIGNIFICANT EVENTS;B. FORCES STATUS;C. C2 ARRANGEMENTS (OPCOM/OPCON/TACOM/TACON);D. OPERATION SITUATION BY AREA (PATROLS, FIRINGS, LAYING OF ADDITIONAL MINES, BARRIERS, OBSTACLES);E. RAPID REACTION FORCES;F. RISK ASSESSMENT;G. COMPLIANCE.ADDITIONAL INSTRUCTIONS FOR SET 13, AIR OPERATIONS.  INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING ITEMS:A. SIGNIFICANT EVENTS;B. AIRFIELD STATUS;C. FORCES STATUS;D. C2 ARRANGEMENTS;E. OPERATIONS SUMMARY (AEW, FIGHTER, RECCE SORTIES, ETC.);F. AEW SORTIES SCHEDULED, FLOWN, CANCELLED, TOTAL, BY NATION;G. SUMMARY OF FIGHTER SORTIES SCHEDULED, FLOWN, CANCELLED, TOTAL, BY NATION;  (1) NFZ CAP SORTIES;  (2) FIGHTER TRAINING SORTIES;H. SUMMARY OF DF OAS SORTIES SCHEDULED, FLOWN, CANCELLED, TOTAL, BY NATION;  (1) DF OAS SORTIES;  (2) OAS TRAINING SORTIES;  (3) ABCCC SORTIES;  (4) AC-130 GUNSHIP SORTIES,I. SUMMARY OF RECONNAISSANCE (RECCE) SORTIES SCHEDULED, FLOWN, CANCELLED, TOTAL, BY NATION;  (1) RECCE SORTIES;  (2) RECCE TRAINING SORTIES;J. SUMMARY OF DF REFUELLING SORTIES SCHEDULED, FLOWN, CANCELLED, TOTAL, BY NATION;K. AIR DEFFENSE GROUND ALERT;L. RISK ASSESSMENT;M. COMPLIANIES.SHAPE WILL SUBMIT THE SITREP, DAILY NLT 0700Z, TO NATO SITCEN AND NMRS AT SHAPE CONCERNED.

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
