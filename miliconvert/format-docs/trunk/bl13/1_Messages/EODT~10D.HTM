EODTASKREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

EODTASKREP (EOD TASKING / EOD REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

AEODP-6
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE EODTASKREP IS USED TO ASSIGN TASKS TO EXPLOSIVE ORDNANCE TEAMS, CONFIRM THE IDENTIFICATION OF UNEXPLODED ORDNANCE (UXO), PROVIDE ADDITIONAL EXPLOSIVE ORDNANCE DISPOSAL (EOD) INFORMATION, DISPOSAL ACTIONS AND RESULTS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) EXPLOSIVE ORDNANCE DISPOSAL INTERSERVICE WORKING GROUP (EODWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
