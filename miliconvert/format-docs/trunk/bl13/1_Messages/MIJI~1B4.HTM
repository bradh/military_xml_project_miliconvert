MIJIWARNREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

MIJIWARNREP (MIJI WARNING REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2423
STANAG 6004 (ED 3)

 

 

Purpose:

THE MIJIWARNREP IS USED IN TIMES OF PEACE AND CRISIS TO WARN NATO NATIONS, COMMANDS AND UNITS OF HAZARDOUS ELECTRONIC WARFARE SITUATIONS CAUSED BY MEACONING, INTRUSION, JAMMING, AND INTERFERENCE (MIJI) INCIDENTS, WHICH ARE OF HOSTILE, FRIENDLY (INADVERTENT) OR UNKNOWN ORIGIN.

 

 

Sponsor:

NATO ELECTRONIC WARFARE ADVISORY COMMITTEE (NEWAC)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
