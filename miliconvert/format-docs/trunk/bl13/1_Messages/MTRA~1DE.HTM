MTRANSIT

 

Message identifier (Name):

MTRANSIT (MINE COUNTERMEASURES TRANSIT INSTRUCTIONS)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1 (C) VOL I
ATP-24

 

 

Purpose:

THE MTRANSIT IS USED TO PROMULGATE INSTRUCTIONS/INFORMATION TO NAVAL FORCES AND/OR CONVOY COMMANDERS CONCERNING MINE COUNTERMEASURES OPERATIONS AND THEIR IMPACT ON CHANNEL PASSAGES.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) NAVAL MINE WARFARE WORKING GROUP (NMWWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
