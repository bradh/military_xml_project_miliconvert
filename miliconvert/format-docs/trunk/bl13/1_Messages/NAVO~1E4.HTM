NAVOPDEF

 

Message identifier (Name):

NAVOPDEF (NAVAL OPERATIONAL DEFECT)

 

 

Related Documents:

ATP-1

 

 

Purpose:

THE NAVOPDEF IS USED TO INFORM THE CHAIN OF COMMAND OF RESTRICTIONS ON THE OPERATIONAL CAPABILITY OF UNITS OF THE FLEET, THE SHIP'S INTENTIONS FOR REPAIR AND TO INITIATE REPAIR ASSISTANCE OR ADVICE AS APPROPRIATE.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

STRUCTURED LANGUAGE/NOTATION ENSURES THAT FOR AN INITIAL OPDEF MESSAGE THE FOLLOWING SETS ARE MANDATORY:OPDEFDET, DEFEQUIP, DEFPART, GENTEXT (DESCRIPTION OF DEFECT), REPAIR, GENTEXT (REPAIR PROPOSAL), ASISTREQ, STOREDEM.GENTEXT SETS THAT IMMEDIATELY FOLLOW THE OPDEFDET, ASISTREQ, STOREDEM SETS ARE ASSOCIATED WITH THE PRECEDING SET AND MAY ONLY BE COMPLETED IF THAT SET IS INCLUDED IN THE MESSAGE.

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
