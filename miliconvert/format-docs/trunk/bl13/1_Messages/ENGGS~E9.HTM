ENGGSORD

 

Message identifier (Name):

ENGGSORD (GENERAL ENGINEER SUPPORT EXECUTION ORDER)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2430 (AENGRP-2 (A))

 

 

Purpose:

THE ENGGSORD IS USED TO ORDER THE EXECUTION OF GENERAL ENGINEER SUPPORT TASKS OR THOSE TASKS WHICH DO NOT FALL UNDER THE CATEGORIES OF MOBILITY, COUNTER-MOBILITY AND SURVIVABILITY.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) COMBAT ENGINEER WORKING GROUP (ENGRWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
