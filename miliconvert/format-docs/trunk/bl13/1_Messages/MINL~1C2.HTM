MINLAYORD

 

Message identifier (Name):

MINLAYORD (MINEFIELD LAYING ORDER)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2430 (AENGRP-2 (A))

 

 

Purpose:

THE MINLAYORD IS USED TO ORDER MINEFIELD LAYING OPERATIONS BY FRIENDLY FORCES.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) COMBAT ENGINEER WORKING GROUP (ENGRWG)

 

 

Notes:

SET SEQUENCE 31:  RESPONSIBILITY FOR ACTIVITATING THE MINEFIELD COVERS THE LAYING OF ALL TYPES OF MINES AND REFERS TO MINES BEING LAID ARMED, OR BEING LAID AND SUBSEQUENTLY ARMED BY REMOTE CONTROL.

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
