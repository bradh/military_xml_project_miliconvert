OPTASK EW

 

Message identifier (Name):

OPTASK EW (OPERATIONAL TASKING ELECTRONIC WARFARE)

 

 

Related Documents:

ACP 176
ACP 178
ATP-1
NATO EMITTER DATA BASE (NEDB)

 

 

Purpose:

THE OPTASK EW IS USED TO PROMULGATE DETAILED TASKING AND INSTRUCTIONS FOR THE CONDUCT OF ELECTRONIC WARFARE.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (NAVY) MARITIME OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (MAROPS WG)

 

 

Notes:

NOTES FOR SET GENTEXT/SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS:  MAY INCLUDE DETECTION, CLASSIFICATION, TARGETING, NON STANDARD OCCASIONS FOR BREAKING EMCON, MIJI, LASER INCIDENTS, IREPS REPORTING SHIPS, ORGANIC EW MISSION AIRCRAFT, NON ORGANIC EW MISSION AIRCRAFT, EW EQUIPMENT READINESS POSTURE OR JOINT/ALLIED EW COORDINATION INFORMATION.

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
