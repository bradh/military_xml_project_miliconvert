INTSUM

 

Message identifier (Name):

INTSUM (INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY)

 

 

Related Documents:

STANAG 2511

 

 

Purpose:

THE INTSUM IS USED TO PERIODICALLY INFORM ADDRESSEES OF MILITARY, ASSOCIATED POLITICAL/ECONOMICAL OR OTHER RELATED INTELLIGENCE AND THE ASSESSMENT OF THIS. IT GIVES AN INDICATION OF CHANGE IN POTENTIAL OPFOR (OPPOSING FORCE) CAPABILITIES, PREPAREDNESS OR MILITARY POSTURE, ACTIVITIES, INTENTIONS, OBJECTIVES AND/OR COURSES OF ACTION IN PEACE, OPERATIONS OTHER THAN WAR, AND WAR.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (JSB) JOINT INTELLIGENCE WORKING GROUP (JINTWG)

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
