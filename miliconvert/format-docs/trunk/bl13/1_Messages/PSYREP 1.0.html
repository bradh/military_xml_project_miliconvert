PSYREP

 

Message identifier (Name):

PSYREP (PSYCHOLOGICAL OPERATIONS REPORT)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE, VOL. III
STANAG 2434

 

 

Purpose:

THE PSYREP IS USED TO INFORM SACEUR AND OTHER ACE COMMANDERS DURING PERIODS OF TENSION AND WAR OF CAPABILITIES AND PLANNED OPERATIONS RELATING TO PSYCHOLOGICAL OPERATIONS.

 

 

Sponsor:

NSA (ARMY) LAND OPERATIONS WORKING GROUP (LOWG) SHAPE

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
