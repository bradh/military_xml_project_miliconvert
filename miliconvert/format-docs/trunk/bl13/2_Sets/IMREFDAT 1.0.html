IMAGERY SCALE

IMAGERY SCALE NUMERIC

PERCENT OF COVERAGE

MAJOR WEATHER CONDITION

ENVIRONMENTAL EFFECTS ON IMAGES

IMAGERY MISSION DATE, YYYYMMDD
