Designator

Field Desc

Concept/Explanation/Examples

1

HOSTILE BATTERY DIRECTION INDICATION

 

ENTER THE DIRECTION TO THE SUSPECTED HOSTILE BATTERY IN ONE OF THE FOLLOWING WAYS:

 

 

1A

OBSERVER-FLASH SOUND AZIMUTH TO HOSTILE BATTERY IN MILS

 

ENTER THE AZIMUTH IN MILS (0000-6399) TO THE MUZZLE FLASH OR ACOUSTIC SIGNATURE, FOR EXAMPLE: "1345". See table 1048/13 a range [0000 through 6399]

 

 

1B

DIRECTION OF SHELL GROOVE IN MILS

DSG

ENTER THE FIELD DESCRIPTOR "DSG:" FOLLOWED BY THE AZIMUTH IN MILS (0000-6399) DERIVED FROM THE ANALYSIS OF THE SHELL CRATER OR GROOVE, FOR EXAMPLE: "DSG:3675". See table 1048/12 a range [0000 through 6399]

2

DISTANCE IN METRES FROM OBSERVER TO HOSTILE BATTERY

 

 

 

 

2A

DISTANCE IN METRES FROM OBSERVER TO HOSTILE BATTERY

 

ENTER THE DISTANCE IN METRES TO THE HOSTILE BATTERY FROM THE OBSERVERS LOCATION, FOR EXAMPLE: "6400". See table 1052/5 a range [1 through 99999]

3

FLASH-TO-BANG TIME IN SECONDS

 

 

 

 

3A

FLASH-TO-BANG TIME IN SECONDS

 

ENTER THE TIME IN SECONDS FROM OBSERVATION OF THE FLASH UNTIL THE SOND OF THE FIRING WAS HEARD BY THE OBSERVER, FOR EXAMPLE: "8". See table 1073/3 a range [0 through 999]
