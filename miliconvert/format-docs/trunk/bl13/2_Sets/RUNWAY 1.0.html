MAXIMUM BEARING WEIGHT

 

THE BEARING WEIGHT OF THE RUNWAY EXPRESSED IN KILOGRAMS OR TONS.

 

 

5A

WEIGHT IN KILOGRAMS

KG

ENTER "KG:" FOLLOWED BY THE WEIGHT IN KILOGRAMS, FOR EXAMPLE: "KG:300" See table 1194/1 a range [1 through 9999]
