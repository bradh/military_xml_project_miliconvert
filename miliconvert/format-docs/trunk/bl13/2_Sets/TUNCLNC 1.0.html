1A

MINIMUM CLEAR WIDTH (FEET, YARDS OR METRES)

HC

ENTER THE MINIMUM CLEAR WIDTH BETWEEN THE INSIDE SURFACES/WALLS OF THE TUNNEL, TAKING ACCOUNT OF HORIZONTAL PROJECTIONS OVER THE ROADWAY, UPWARDS FROM A HEIGHT OF 30 CENTIMETRES (OR 1 FOOT) ABOVE THE ROADWAY SURFACE, MEASURED IN EITHER FEET, YARDS OR METRES, FOR EXAMPLE: "HC:10M". See table 2451 which is a composite

2

WIDTH OF TRAVELED WAY (FEET, YARDS OR METRES)

 

 

 

 

2A

WIDTH OF TRAVELED WAY (FEET, YARDS OR METRES)

TW

ENTER THE MINIMUM DISTANCE BETWEEN THE CURBS JUST ABOVE THE TRAVELLED WAY; BELOW 30 CENTIMETRES OR 1 FOOT, MEASURED IN FEET, YARDS OR METRES, FOR EXAMPLE: "TW:9M". See table 2451 which is a composite

3

MINIMUM CLEAR HEIGHT/CLEARANCE

 

 

 

 

3A

MINIMUM CLEAR HEIGHT/CLEARANCE

OC

ENTER THE MINIMUM DISTANCE BETWEEN THE TRAVELLED WAY AND ANY OBSTRUCTION ABOVE IT. MEASUREMENTS ARE TAKEN AT THE LEFT SHOULDER, CENTRE OF ROADWAY, AND THE RIGHT SHOULDER.  IF ALL MEASUREMENTS ARE EQUAL, REPORT ONLY ONCE.  MEASUREMENTS ARE EITHER IN FEET, YARDS OR METRES, FOR EXAMPLE: "OC:10M".
