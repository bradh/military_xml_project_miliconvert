3A

MAX NUMBER HEAVY AIRLIFT AIRCRAFT ON GROUND

 

IF USED, ENTER THE MAXIMUM NUMBER (1 THROUGH 999) OF HEAVY AIRCRAFT (VC10, B707, C141) THAT MAY BE ON THE GROUND AT EITHER THE ONLOAD OR OFFLOAD BASE, FOR EXAMPLE: "10". See table 1044/173 a range [1 through 999]

4

MAX NUMBER MEDIUM AIRLIFT AIRCRAFT ON GROUND

 

MUST BE USED, IF MAX NUMBER HEAVY AIRLIFT AIRCRAFT ON GROUND HAS NOT BEEN SPECIFIED.

 

 

4A

MAX NUMBER MEDIUM AIRLIFT AIRCRAFT ON GROUND

 

IF USED, ENTER THE MAXIMUM NUMBER (1 THROUGH 999) OF MEDIUM AIRCRAFT (C130, C160, G222) THAT MAY BE ON THE GROUND AT EITHER THE ONLOAD OR OFFLOAD BASE, FOR EXAMPLE: "22". See table 1044/175 a range [1 through 999]
