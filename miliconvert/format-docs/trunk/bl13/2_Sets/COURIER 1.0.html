DATE AND TIME INDICATOR

 

ENTER THE TIME OF ARRIVAL IN ONE OF THREE WAYS:

 

 

4A

DAY-TIME AND MONTH OF ARRIVAL

DATE AND TIME OF DEPARTURE

 

ENTER THE DATE AND TIME OF DEPARTURE IN ONE THREE WAYS:

 

 

6A

DAY-TIME AND MONTH OF DEPARTURE

 

A: ENTER THE DAY OF A MONTH AND TIMEKEEPING IN HOURS AND MINUTES OF A CALENDAR DAY, USING THE 24-HOUR CLOCK SYSTEM, ASSOCIATED TIME ZONE AND MONTH, FOR EXAMPLE: "241045ZMAR". See table 2030 which is a composite

 

 

6B

DEPARTURE REFERENCE TIME INDICATOR
