TIME RELATIVE TO H-HOUR IN MINUTES

 

 

 

 

1A

TIME RELATIVE TO H-HOUR IN MINUTES

 

ONE TO FOUR CHARACTERS USED TO SPECIFY THE SCHEDULED TIME ON TARGET (TOT) RELATIVE TO H-HOUR.A.  ONE TO THREE DIGITS WILL SPECIFY THE MINUTES AFTER H-HOUR, FOR EXAMPLE: "15".B.  TO SPECIFY THE MINUTES PRIOR TO H-HOUR ENTER "-" (MINUS) FOLLOWED BY ONE TO THREE DIGITS, FOR EXAMPLE: "-35".C.  ENTER "0" (ZERO) TO INDICATE THE TOT IS AT H-HOUR. See table 1072/22 a range [-240 through 240]

2

DURATION OF FIRE IN MINUTES RELATIVE TO H-HOUR

 

 

 

 

2A

DURATION OF FIRE IN MINUTES RELATIVE TO H-HOUR

 

ENTER ONE TO TWO DIGITS TO SPECIFY THE NUMBER OF MINUTES THE TARGET IS TO BE ENGAGED, FOR EXAMPLE: "5". See table 1072/23 a range [1 through 99]
