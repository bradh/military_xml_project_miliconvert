OVERALL LENGTH IN METRES, 1-99.99

OVERALL WING SPAN IN METRES

 

ENTER THE OVERALL WIDTH OF THE AIRCRAFT IN METRES, FOR EXAMPLE: "8.5". See table 1658/22 a range [1 through 99.99] (0 to 2 decimal places)
