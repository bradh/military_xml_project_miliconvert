1

RIGHT BANK HEIGHT IN METRES

 

Enter either the bank height above the water or above ground level.

 

 

1A

RIGHT BANK HEIGHT ABOVE WATER LEVEL IN METRES

HAW

ENTER "HAW:" FOLLOWED BY THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE WATER LEVEL AND THE BANK HEIGHT ON THE RIGHT BANK OF THE WATER COURSE, FOR EXAMPLE: "0.40". See table 1651/9 a range [0 through 99.9] (0 to 1 decimal places)

 

 

1B

RIGHT BANK HEIGHT ABOVE GROUND LEVEL IN METRES

 

DESCRIBES THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE GROUND LEVEL AND THE BANK HEIGHT ON THE RIGHT BANK OF THE WATER COURSE, FOR EXAMPLE: "0.40". See table 1651/14 a range [0 through 99.9] (0 to 1 decimal places)
