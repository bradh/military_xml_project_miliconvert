5A

GRADIENT

GRD

ENTER "GRD:" AND THE INFORMATION DEFINING THE GRADIENT OF THE AIR LANDING ZONE, EXPRESSED AS A RATIO OF DROP OR RISE IN THE TERRAIN.  THE NUMBER OF UNITS FORWARD TO ACHIEVE ONE UNIT OF DROP OR RISE (ZERO REPRESENTS A LEVEL LANDING ZONE), FOR EXAMPLE: "GRD:20". See table 1789/1 a range [0 through 99]

SURFACE COVERING OF THE LANDING OR DROP ZONE

8

SUB-SURFACE COVERING OF THE LANDING OR DROP ZONE

 

 

 

 

8A

SUB-SURFACE COVERING OF THE LANDING OR DROP ZONE

 

ENTER THE TYPE OF SUB-SURFACE COVERING FOUND AT THE LANDING ZONE, FOR EXAMPLE: "EARTH". THIS FIELD IS MANDATORY IF THE ENTRY IN FIELD 7 IS "SNOW" OR "ICE". See table 1783/2 which contains a list of data items and associated data codes

 

 

Structured Notation:

[^]F8 RP ([^]F7 EQ /SNOW/ | /ICE/ | /DUST/)

 

 

Structured Language:      

FIELD 8 IN SET ^ (ALZDESC) IS REQUIRED IF FIELD 7 IN SET ^ (ALZDESC) EQUALS /SNOW/ OR /ICE/ OR /DUST/, OTHERWISE IT IS PROHIBITED.
