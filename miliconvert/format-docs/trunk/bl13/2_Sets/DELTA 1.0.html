DATE-TIME GROUP OF ATTACK OR DETONATION

 

PROVIDES THE DATE-TIME GROUP OF ATTACK OR DETONATION IN ONE OF THE FOLLOWING WAYS:

 

 

1A

DTG OF ATTACK, ZULU

ATT

ENTER "ATT:" FOLLOWED BY THE DATE-TIME GROUP OF ATTACK, FOR EXAMPLE: "ATT:191200ZJUL2003". See table 2323 which is a composite

 

 

1B

DTG OF DETONATION, ZULU

DET

ENTER "DET:" FOLLOWED BY THE DATE-TIME GROUP OF DETONATION, FOR EXAMPLE: "DET:191430ZDEC2001". See table 2323 which is a composite
