No

Designator

Field Desc

Concept/Explanation/Examples

1

REPORTABLE ITEM CODE

 

ALLOWS FOR REPORTABLE ITEM CODE OR AN EXPANDED IDENTIFIER, I.E. AN NSN PLUS ADDITIONAL ID.

 

 

1A

REPORTABLE ITEM CODE (EXPANDED)

 

ENTER THE LITERAL CODE FROM LOGREP SOFTWARE/RIC SYSTEM OR FROM MISSION TAILORED REPORTABLE ITEM LIST (RIL) PROVIDED BY SC/DESIGNATED COMMAND.  IF NO RIC IS ASSIGNED ENTER THE NATIONAL ID CODE (NIC) OR NATO STOCK NUMBER TO THE REPORTED ITEM, FOR EXAMPLE "MC25CB". See table 1281/2 which is an instructive entry

2

SHORT TITLE

 

THE SHORT TITLE OF THE ITEM BEING REPORTED.  SEE LOGREP SOFTWARE/RIC SYSTEM, OR RIL PROVIDED BY SC/DESIGNATED COMMAND.

 

 

2A

SHORT TITLE (EXPANDED)

 

ENTER THE SHORT TITLE OF ITEM BEING REPORTED.  SEE LOGREP SOFTWARE/RIC SYSTEM, OR RIL PROVIDED BY SC/DESIGNATED COMMAND, EXAMPLE "BMB-MK84-RE". See table 1022/302 which is an instructive entry

3

UNIT OF MEASUREMENT

 

UNIT OF MEASUREMENT USED FOR THE REPORTED ITEM.

 

 

3A

UNIT OF MEASUREMENT, STOCK ITEM

 

A UNIT OF LINEAR, VOLUME, WEIGHT, OR PACKING MEASUREMENT IN WHICH THE FOLLOWING QUANTITIES WILL BE REPORTED, FOR EXAMPLE "EA". See table 1008/51 which contains a list of data items and associated data codes

4

REQUIRED TOTAL

 

TOTAL QUANTITY OF AN ITEM REQUIRED TO BE ON-HAND AND IN RESERVE.

 

 

4A

REQUIRED TOTAL

 

ENTER THE TOTAL QUANTITY OF AN ITEM REQUIRED TO BE HELD ON-HAND AND IN RESERVE BY A FORMATION/UNIT TO MEET THE NATO ASSIGNED TASK IN ACCORDANCE WITH FORCE STANDARDS OR ESTABLISHED MISSION REQUIREMENTS.  QUANTITIES AS DECLARED BY NATIONS IN THE DPQ REPLY, WHEN APPLICABLE, OR AS DECLARED FOR NATIONAL UNITS IN PLANS/OPERATIONS.  ENTER THE CONTEXT QUANTITY (0-99999999), FOR EXAMPLE "150". See table 1853/2 a range [0 through 99999999.0000000] (0 to 7 decimal places)

5

REQUIRED ON-HAND

 

QUANTITY OF ITEM REQUIRED TO BE HELD BY A UNIT IN ACCORDANCE WITH FORCE STANDARDS.

 

 

5A

REQUIRED ON HAND

 

ENTER THE QUANTITY OF AN ITEM REQUIRED TO BE HELD ON-HAND BY A FORMATION/UNIT (NOT INCLUDING ITEMS IN RESERVE) IN ACCORDANCE WITH FORCE STANDARDS/TASK REQUIREMENTS.  ENTER THE CONTEXT QUANTITY (0-99999999), FOR EXAMPLE "110". See table 1853/3 a range [0 through 99999999.0000000] (0 to 7 decimal places)

6

ACTUAL ON HAND

 

 

 

 

6A

ACTUAL ON HAND

 

ENTER THE QUANTITY OF ITEMS PHYSICALLY HELD ON HAND BY FORMATION/UNIT (FOR NATIONAL REPLIES: ON-HAND HOLDINGS PLUS RESERVES) AS FOLLOWS: ENTER THE CONTEXT QUANTITY (0-99999999), FOR EXAMPLE "85". See table 1853/6 a range [0 through 99999999.0000000] (0 to 7 decimal places)

7

TOTAL DUE IN

 

THE QUANTITY OF ITEMS EXPECTED TO BE RECEIVED BEFORE THE NEXT REPORTING PERIOD.

 

 

7A

TOTAL DUE IN

 

ENTER THE QUANTITY OF ITEMS EXPECTED TO BE RECEIVED BY FORMATION/UNIT BEFORE THE NEXT REPORTING PERIOD: ENTER THE CONTEXT QUANTITY (0-99999999), FOR EXAMPLE "20". See table 1853/4 a range [0 through 99999999.0000000] (0 to 7 decimal places)

8

TOTAL DUE OUT

 

THE QUANTITY OF ITEMS EXPECTED TO BE TRANSFERRED OUT BEFORE THE NEXT REPORTING PERIOD.

 

 

8A

TOTAL DUE OUT

 

ENTER THE QUANTITY OF ITEMS EXPECTED TO BE TRANSFERRED/DELIVERED OUT OF THE FORMATION/UNIT BEFORE THE NEXT REPORTING PERIOD (NOT CONSUMPTION): ENTER THE CONTEXT QUANTITY (0-99999999),  FOR EXAMPLE "5". See table 1853/5 a range [0 through 99999999.0000000] (0 to 7 decimal places)

9

REQUIRED STOCKS CALCULATION

 

CODE SPECIFYING THE REQUIRED TOTAL STOCKS CALCULATION METHOD.

 

 

9A

REQUIRED STOCKS CALCULATION METHOD

 

ENTER THE CODE SPECIFYING THE REQUIRED TOTAL STOCKS CALCULATION METHOD WHEN REQUESTED FOR ACROSS SOFTWARE PURPOSES, OTHERWISE NOT APPLICABLE.   FOR EXAMPLE "LOE" (ALT: "TRM"). See table 1568/2 which contains a list of data items and associated data codes

10

LIMITING FACTORS/REMARKS

 

IN FREE TEXT, THE LOGISTICS FACTORS WHICH ARE DEGRADING THE OPERATIONAL CAPABILITY OF A FORMATION AND/OR ANY SUBSTANTIAL REMARKS TO THE ITEM BEING REPORTED.

 

 

10A

LIMITING FACTORS REMARKS

 

ENTER THE LOGISTICS FACTORS WHICH ARE DEGRADING THE OPERATIONAL CAPABILITY OF A FORMATION/UNIT, STATUS ON SERVICEABILITY, AND/OR ANY SUBSTANTIAL REMARKS TO THE ITEMS BEING REPORTED WHEN APPROPRIATE.  IF RPTITEM IS NOT SPECIFIED BY A RIC AND NOT SHORT TITLED BY RIL, ENTER SHORT DESCRIPTION FOR IDENTIFICATION AND CLASSIFICATION OF ITEM INTO THE RIC SYSTEM.  FOR EXAMPLE "INDUSTRIAL PROCUREMENT STARTED". See table 1357/30 which is an instructive entry
