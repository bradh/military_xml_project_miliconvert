BATTERY/COMPANY

005
BATTALION/REGIMENT
( 1-3 AN )
REFERS TO AN ARTILLERY BATTALION IN MANY NATIONAL ARMIES OR ARTILLERY REGIMENT IN THE BRITISH AND CANADIAN ARMIES, WHICH ARE ORGANIZATIONALLY ON THE SAME LEVEL. MAY ALSO BE USED TO REFER TO MANOEUVRE UNITS TO WHICH ARTILLERY OBSERVERS AND LIAISON TEAMS MAY BE ASSIGNED.
006
REGIMENT/BRIGADE/DIVISION
( 1-3 AN )
REFERS TO ARTILLERY REGIMENTS IN NATIONS OTHER THEN THE UK AND CA, ARTILLERY BRIGADES AND DIVISION ARTILLERY WHICH ARE ORGANIZATIONALLY ON THE SAME LEVEL (CONTAINING 2 OR MORE ARTILLERY BATTALIONS OR ARTILLERY REGIMENTS (UK & CA)). MAY ALSO BE USED TO REFER TO MANOEUVRE UNITS TO WHICH ARTILLERY OBSERVERS AND LIAISON TEAMS MAY BE ASSIGNED.
007
CORPS TROOPS OR DIVISION DESIGNATOR

CORPS LEVEL ASSETS OR DIVISIONS.
008
DIVISION TROOPS OR BRIGADE DESIGNATOR
( 1 N )
A DESIGNATOR ASSIGNED BY A DIVISION TO UNIQUELY IDENTIFY EACH OF ITS
DIVISION LEVEL ASSETS OR BRIGADES.
