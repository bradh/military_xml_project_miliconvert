DTG, 4-DIGIT YEAR

DEFINITION
:
A DATE-TIME GROUP (DTG) OF SIX DIGITS WITH A ZONE TIME SUFFIX AND THE STANDARDIZED ABBREVIATION FOR THE MONTH AND A 4-DIGIT YEAR. THE FIRST PAIR OF THE SIX DIGITS REPRESENTS THE DAY; THE SECOND PAIR THE HOUR; THE THIRD PAIR THE MINUTES.
