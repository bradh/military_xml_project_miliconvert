DEFINITION
:
THE COMMON NAMES OF THE SPECTRAL COLOURS. IN MILITARY USAGE, COLOUR NAMES MAY BE USED FOR SIGNALLING PURPOSES (MARKING PANELS, SMOKE, FLARES, ETC.), FOR DESIGNATING SPECIFIC ITEMS OF A GENERAL NATURE (RED BEACH, ROUTE GREEN, RED TEAM, ETC.), AS WELL AS FOR DESCRIBING THE
PERCEIVED VISUAL CHARACTERISTIC OF COLOUR OF OBJECTS OR PHENOMENA.
